## What do I need to publish?

All you need to publish is a MagCloud account and a PDF. Publishers who want to add a markup to their MagCloud publication's price must also have a [PayPal account](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq/item/1012) in order for MagCloud to pay earnings from your sales. See our [Terms of Use](https://www.magcloud.com/about/Terms) for additional information.
