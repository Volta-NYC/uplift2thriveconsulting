HOSEA Magazine is a photo-led publication celebrating Harlem style, beauty, and culture through bold portraiture and intimate storytelling. This premiere issue features the cover story “Cotton Comes to Harlem”—a powerful conversation with street vendor and jewelry artist SLAVERY™, whose cotton-and-copper creations transform history into wearable art.

Inside, you’ll find a music spotlight on soul singer Jrome Andre, beauty and skincare features, actor and fashion portraits, and a closing street-photography moment honoring Malcolm X—all curated with Hosea Johnson’s signature visual language: elegant, direct, and unapologetically human.

Printed in full color and designed for collectors, HOSEA Magazine is a keepsake for anyone who loves portrait photography, Harlem creativity, and stories that live beyond the page.
