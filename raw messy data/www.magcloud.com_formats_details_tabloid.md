- The trim can vary slightly, so make sure full page images fill the bleed area and keep text within the safe zone.

- Don't include printer marks in your PDF, and don’t lock it with a password.

- The first page in your PDF will be your front cover and the last page will be the back cover.

- A 6mm barcode will be added to the lower right-hand corner of your back cover.
- Holes will be punched on the inside edge of all pages for the Wire-O binding.

- The upload size limit for your MagCloud PDF is 300MB.


Print File Checklist:

-     Images at 300dpi
-     Embedded color profiles
-     Embedded fonts
-     Text in CMYK color space
-     No spot colors
