# My Health Record

## Your My Health Record is a secure online summary of your [health information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/health-information/what-is-health-information). The Australian Digital Health Agency runs the [My Health Record](https://www.myhealthrecord.gov.au/) system. We oversee the privacy aspects of My Health Record.
