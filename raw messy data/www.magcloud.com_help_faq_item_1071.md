## What if I opt-in to digital distribution for my MagCloud publications, but later change my mind?

You can opt-out of digital distribution at any time by clicking Edit Publication on your [My Projects](https://www.magcloud.com/publish) page or from the toolbar on your publication page, however any copies that have been purchased or downloaded by readers are theirs to keep - just like physical copies.
