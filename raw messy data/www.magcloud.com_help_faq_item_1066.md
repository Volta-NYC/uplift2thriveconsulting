## Are there different design requirements for digital distribution?

Nope! Just create your publication according to our [PDF guidelines](https://www.magcloud.com/create/resources) and we'll do the rest. If you'd like to add hyperlinks to your digital version to make it more interactive, please see our [Digital Design](https://www.magcloud.com/create/pdf-resources/digitaldesign) page for tips.
