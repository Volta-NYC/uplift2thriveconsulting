# Consumer Data Right complaints

## The Consumer Data Right (CDR) is designed to keep your data secure, with strict privacy protections built into the system.

If you’re an individual or small business with an annual turnover of $3 million or less, and you think a business has mishandled your CDR data, you have the right to complain.

## To complain to us

Use our form to complain to us about the handling of your CDR data: [CDR complaint form](https://forms.oaic.gov.au/forms/complaint "Make a CDR complaint to us")

See also [Your complaint review rights](https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/privacy-complaints/your-complaint-review-rights "Your complaint review rights") about your right to appeal our decision or determination.
