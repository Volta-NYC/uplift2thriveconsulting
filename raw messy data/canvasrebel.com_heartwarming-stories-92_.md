In our conversations with thousands of entrepreneurs, professionals, and creatives we often hear the most heartwarming stories. These stories matter and they show why pursing your passion matters. When you care about what you do, you can make miracles happen for your clients, employees and other stakeholders. You’ll find some amazing stories below.

## Chelsea Gll![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778762440597-personal_1778762440463_1778762440463_chelsea_gll_627651225_2961433980711790_6496601453579288736_n.jpg)

March 1st 2023 as I was running errands and visiting friends of The Buffalo Fire Department, tones went out for a fire downtown Buffalo. A normal day for me to hear tones, as a family member and wife of a first responder. What came next was the unimaginable. During the fire, a firefighter was unaccounted for and the commotion on the scanner was heartbreaking. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-chelsea-gll)

## Jodie Smith

## ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778790037553-personal_1778790037413_1778790037413_jodie_smith_untitled-design.jpg)

I have been in the healthcare industry for over 18 years. When I first started my insurance agency, I was focused on being in the community to help people access quality care at an affordable cost. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jodie-smith-2)

## Eugene Bestman![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778458883775-personal_1778458883631_1778458883631_eugene_bestman_cdb54dba-4991-44b0-bf9e-91577c9e2aed.jpg)

One of the most heartwarming moments in my career actually came from a full circle experience that reminded me exactly why I chose oral and maxillofacial surgery in the first place. I grew up in Ghana and experienced dental trauma as a child after an accident that left me with damaged front teeth. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-eugene-bestman)

## Tanya Love![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778652991900-personal_1778652991683_1778652991683_tanya_love_img_6311.jpeg)

One of the most unforgettable patients of my career was a sweet older gentleman who suffered an ischemic stroke affecting the visual cortex of his brain, leaving him completely blind. I first met him about a week after he was discharged from the hospital. Understandably, he was frightened and uncertain about what his future would look like. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tanya-love)

## Pooja Church![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777916432460-personal_1777916431337_1777916431337_pooja_church_untitled-design-9.jpg)

I’ve had the opportunity to coach the most amazing, bad ass women that run teams, businesses, make life/death decisions, all while managing their households, raising their children and deciding to own and earn the body of their dreams. Every woman has both a gut-wrenching and heart-warming story… [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-pooja-church)

## Dulce Luna![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778005563211-personal_1778005563070_1778005563070_dulce_luna_septima.jpg)

In my journey as a wellness professional, I have come to understand that we all go through moments that seem insurmountable: loss, betrayal, or crises that leave the heart stunned. In those instances, loneliness can feel overwhelming—even when surrounded by people—because each person carries the exact weight of their own pain. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-dulce)
