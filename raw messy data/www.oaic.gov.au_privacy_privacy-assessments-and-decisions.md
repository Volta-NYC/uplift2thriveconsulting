# Privacy assessment and decisions

We publish final reports of privacy assessments we conduct on agencies and organisations the _Privacy Act 1988_ covers.

Under privacy decisions we publish information on:

- enforceable undertakings
- investigation reports
- privacy complaint outcomes
- privacy determinations.
