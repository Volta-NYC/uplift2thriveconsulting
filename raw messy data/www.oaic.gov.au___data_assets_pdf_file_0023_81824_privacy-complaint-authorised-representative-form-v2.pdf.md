Privacy Complaint Authorised Representative Form
\[Image: Im0\]

Please note that the OAIC provides an independent and free service. You can avoid
any charges an agent applies in relation to a matter referred to the OAIC by
contacting the OAIC directly.
\[Image: Im0\]

I, (Name of complainant), nominate (Name of representative) to act as my representative in
the attached privacy complaint.
\[Image: Im0\]

I understand that the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) will be
discussing my personal information with my representative in the course of handling my
complaint.
\[Image: Im0\]

I am also aware that information provided by my representative about me to the OAIC, will
be disclosed to the respondent in this matter, with a view to resolving my complaint.
\[Image: Im0\]

I will notify the OAIC directly if I would like to revoke this authority.
\[Image: Im0\]

I agree to have my representative act as sole contact in this matter and I understand that the
OAIC will be communicating with my representative and not myself, during this time.
