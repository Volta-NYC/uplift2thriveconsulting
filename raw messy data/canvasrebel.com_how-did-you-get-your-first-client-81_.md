There are so many varied experiences when it comes to starting a business. From what we’ve seen, there is no one, right answer. Some people found success by writing a business plan, spending months or years perfecting the idea and only then approaching their first client while others found their first client before they had even settled on a name for their business. Below, you’ll find the stories of how entrepreneurs from across industries and markets found their first clients.

The Themed Cuisine, formerly the Brunch Lady was introduced in 2020, during the peak of COVID-19. During this time, enjoying time out at restaurants, were obsolete. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-danielle-mattocks-2)

## Quarina Sultana![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779304368524-personal_1779304368279_1779304368279_quarina_sultana_img_4439-744x1024.jpg)

The first client I got completely independently came through LinkedIn at a point where I was still figuring out whether freelancing was something I could genuinely make work long term. I’d been sharing thoughts online around digital engagement, content and audience journeys alongside my in-house roles, not in a polished “personal brand” way, but more casually posting observations from projects I was working on. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-quarina)

## Muhammad Ibrahim

My first client story isn’t glamorous and I think that’s exactly what makes it worth telling. When I decided I wanted to build something of my own, I had no network, no referrals, and no one handing me opportunities. So I did what most hungry freelancers do, I turned to the marketplaces. Upwork became my battleground. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-muhammad-ibrahim)

## Whitney Sincoff![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778887260106-personal_1778887259978_1778887259978_whitney_sincoff_whitney-final-review-8-683x1024.jpeg)

Much of my business today is the leasing of fine, contemporary art to production companies for tv shows and film sets. My first client in this realm was the television show, ‘How I Met Your Mother.’ At the time, I was renting a loft space above another, different art rental business, and a set decorator came into our offices looking for artwork for the show. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-whitney-sincoff)

## Maria Romano![Meet Maria Romano](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779218835423-personal_1779218835228_1779218835228_maria_romano_v1-0208_maria-photoshoot00000000.jpg)

When I first started my business almost 5 years, I had ZERO experience, zero clients (obviously) and ZERO clue what I was doing if I am being honest. After ONE week of creating an Instagram account to market my business, which was as a Virtual Assistant at the time- I realized there was literally NO way I’d be able to do this alone. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-maria-romano)

## jasmine harris![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778904095279-personal_1778904092620_1778904092620_jasmine_harris_ce105b07-3f7f-4293-b1a6-c6bc2701f00b-683x1024.png)

My first real client came completely by word of mouth, and honestly, it changed everything for me. At the time, I was just focused on painting and perfecting my craft. I wasn’t running ads, I didn’t have a huge social media following, and I definitely didn’t have some big business strategy figured out yet. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jasmine-harris-3)

## Carolyn Ayinla![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779060633547-personal_1779060633417_1779060633417_carolyn_ayinla_carolyn-cover-pic-768x615.jpeg)

When I first started Insight Permit Consulting, I knew I was entering an industry where most business comes from referrals and established relationships. The challenge was that I was brand new as a business owner, so I couldn’t rely on a huge network yet. I had to create opportunities for myself. I’ve honestly been taking photos my whole life. My phone has thousands of pictures because I’ve always had this instinct to capture everything around me. ## Kierra Wilson![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779219766942-personal_1779219766747_1779219766747_kierra_wilson_kierra-headshot-green-blazer-2-732x1024.png)
