Coming up with an idea for a business or creative project can feel exhilarating. Inspiration for a new idea can come from so many places and we’ve asked some great folks from the community to share their stories of how they came up with their ideas.

## David Besgrove![Meet David Besgrove](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779217547078-personal_1779217547301_1779217547301_david_besgrove_img_2322.jpeg)

My degree is in Printing Managment and I was working for a large printing company in Dallas. One of my coworkers made wine at home and he was sharing with me the process. I decided to try this and was not very good. Not giving up, I continued the process and became rather good at producing the wine. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-david-besgrove)

## Anita Allemant![Meet Anita Allemant](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779227713787-personal_1779227713582_1779227713582_anita_allemant_photo-output.jpeg)

At first my idea was born for fun; I was in a convention (Festival del Fumetto at Novegro, Italy) more than 10 years ago, and I fell in love with everything there was; i came up with the idea of sharing that amazing world (cosplay, comics, nerd,…) because 10 years ago in Italy that world was almost unknown. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-anita-allemant)

## Melissa Corbin![Meet Melissa Corbin](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779323035462-personal_1779323035029_1779323035029_melissa_corbin_corbincosmetics-429.jpg)

Corbin Cosmetics was born during one of the most stressful and uncertain seasons of my life. I had always loved aesthetics because it combines medicine, artistry, and confidence-building in such a unique way. Over time, I realized many patients were looking for a different experience. They wanted natural results, honest guidance, and to feel cared for instead of pressured or overdone. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-melissa-corbin-2)

## Maria Volodkevich

I spent 6.5 years serving as an Air Force Intelligence Officer. From the outside, my life looked incredibly aligned. I graduated from the U.S. Air Force Academy, served overseas, and built a career centered around service and purpose. But internally, I was struggling with identity and trying to figure out who I was beyond the uniform. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-maria-volodkevich)

## Nathanael Nunemaker![Meet Nathanael Nunemaker](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779217408985-personal_1779217408787_1779217408787_nathanael_nunemaker_2023_5_15_fns_2258_original.jpeg)

My introduction to hot sauce and spicy foods came during 2020, amid the COVID pandemic. Like most others, work slowed down and I had a lot of free time on my hands. I decided that I wanted to try my hand at growing my own food, which was an absolute blast. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nathanael-nunemaker-4)

## Jeania Pierre![Meet Jeania Pierre](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779077892175-personal_1779077891641_1779077891641_jeania_pierre_9e2bf4a7-6208-494b-b9fb-12ebff394095.png)
