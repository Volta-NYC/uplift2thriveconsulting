## Do you have a volume/bulk discount?

Whenever you buy 10 or more print copies you will receive a discount off the printing costs. This also applies to [Ship to Group orders](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq/item/1032).

|     |     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **1 - 9 copies** | **10 - 49 copies** | **50 - 99 copies** | **100+ copies** |
| Base price | 15% | 20% | 25% |

[Click here](https://www.magcloud.com/products/pricing) to use our cost calculator to see the expected cost for your own order and your customers' orders.

Note that discounts cannot be combined, so in the case of competing discounts - for example, ordering 20 or more copies of something during a site-wide sale - the higher of the two discounts will be applied.
