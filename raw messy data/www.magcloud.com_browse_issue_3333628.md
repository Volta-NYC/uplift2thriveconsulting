Photografique is the magazine for innovative photographers who want their photography to be seen and discovered. Photografique is dedicated to exposing new, emerging, and longstanding photographers to readers around the world through online and print-on-demand sources. Curated photographers are featured with a 2 page gatefold display with headshot, biography, artist statement, and, most important, their original photography in full color. Social media and personal links for each artist are included.
