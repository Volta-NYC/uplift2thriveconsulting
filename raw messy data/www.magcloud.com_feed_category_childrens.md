## [Into The Mirror](https://www.magcloud.com/browse/issue/3335158)

Through The Mirror is a magical fantasy about finding where you truly belong. The story follows Daisy, a girl who always felt out of place especially around mirrors. Until one stormy night where is pulled through into an enchanted kingdom. Alongside her is another version of herself—“the other Daisy”—who eventually confesses that their lives were exchanged as children. Daisy was born in the mirror world, while the other Daisy truly belonged in the human world. Together, they must return both worlds to balance and reclaim the lives stolen from them.

The book blends whimsical fantasy similar to Through the Looking-Glass with emotional themes, enchanted kingdoms, eerie mirror magic, and hauntingly beautiful imagery. It has the feeling of a fairy tale mixed with emotional fantasy adventure, focusing on self-discovery, grief, courage, and the idea that home is not always where you started—but where your soul belongs.
