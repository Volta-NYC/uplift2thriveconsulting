## Online forms

For information on how we handle your information, see our [privacy policy](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/about-us/our-corporate-information/key-documents/privacy-policy).

**Note:** When submitting documents via our online forms over 15MB, please split into multiple documents to under 15MB.

### General enquiry

Use this form to make a [general enquiry](https://webform.oaic.gov.au/prod?entitytype=Enquiry&layoutcode=EnquiryWF) to the OAIC, if you prefer to make your enquiry in writing. This form is not for complaints or reviews.

### Complaints

#### Privacy

Use this form to [make a complaint about the handling of your personal information](https://webform.oaic.gov.au/prod?entitytype=Complaint&layoutcode=ComplaintWF) by an Australian Government agency or a private sector organisation. [More information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/privacy-complaints).

#### Freedom of information

Use this form to [complain about an agency’s actions](https://webform.oaic.gov.au/prod?entitytype=Complaint&layoutcode=FOIComplaintWF) under the _Freedom of information Act 1982_. [More information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/freedom-of-information/reviews-and-complaints).

#### Consumer Data Right

Use this form to make a complaint about the handling of your [Consumer Data Right (CDR) data](https://forms.oaic.gov.au/forms/complaint) by a business you have requested it from or the business you are sending it to, under the CDR system. [More information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/consumer-data-right/cdr-complaints).

### Data breaches

#### Notifiable data breach

Use the [Notifiable data breach](https://webform.oaic.gov.au/prod?entitytype=DBN&layoutcode=DataBreachWF) form to notify the OAIC of an eligible data breach. [More information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/guidance-and-advice/data-breach-preparation-and-response).

### Requests

| Agency freedom of information extension of time request | An Australian Government agency can use this form to [lodge an extension of time to handle a freedom of information request](https://webform.oaic.gov.au/prod?entitytype=ICRequest&layoutcode=ICRequestWF) or to notify of an agreed extension. [More information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/freedom-of-information/freedom-of-information-guidance-for-government-agencies/freedom-of-information-agency-resources/extension-of-time-for-processing-requests). |
| Speech request | Use this form to [request an OAIC speaker \- external site](https://oaic.writemsg.com/em/forms/subscribe.php?db=371540&s=104011&a=57903&k=7538d74) for your event. |

### Reviews

| Freedom of information review | Use this form to [request a review of a freedom of information decision](https://webform.oaic.gov.au/prod?entitytype=ICReview&layoutcode=ICReviewWF) by an Australian Government agency. [More information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/freedom-of-information/reviews-and-complaints). |
