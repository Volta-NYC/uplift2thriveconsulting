**Current version**

**Previous versions**

|  |  | \[2.16\] and \[2.23\] have been updated to include the Independent Parliamentary Standards Commission as an entity now listed under (2AAA) as a result of amendment by the _Parliamentary Workplace Support Service Amendment (Independent Parliamentary Standards Commission) Act 2024_. |

|  |  | \[2.30\] – \[2.31\] have been added to explain that the FOI Act applies in a modified way to some entities even though they are not ‘agencies’ as defined in s 4 of the FOI Act, including the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHRPA) and education and care services agencies. |
