BAYFashion May 2011 brings you the latest update on the Fashion Underground of the San Francisco Bay Area. In this issue we feature the work of designer Danielle Pette whose cupcake themed dresses are perfect for Spring. We also discover the coolest fashion hangouts and stores and provide the most important fashion and beauty tips for our readers. And of course coverage of the hottest fashion shows in the San Francisco Bay continues to be our forte.
