## How many pages can my MagCloud publication have?

The page limit for your MagCloud publication depends on the product you are creating and the binding method you want to use:

For all [Flyer](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/flyer) and [Poster](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/poster) products, the PDF you upload to MagCloud must be 2 pages long.

For all [Pamphlet](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/pamphlet) products, your PDF must be 4 pages long.

For Wire-O Binding, your publication can be as few as 8 pages or as many as 90 pages. The total page count must be a multiple of 2.

For Saddle Stitch Binding, your publication can be as few as 8 pages or as many as 100 pages. The total page count must be a multiple of 4.

For Perfect Binding, your publication can be as few as 24 pages or as many as 384 pages. The total page count must be a multiple of 2.

Learn more about each binding option and the products they are offered with on our [Print Options](https://www.magcloud.com/products/print) page.
