Earning that first dollar is incredibly special and a moment many never forget. We asked some very talented creatives from the community to tell us the story of the first dollar they earned from their creative works.

## Kevin Tellez![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778599848642-personal_1778599848495_1778599848495_kevin_tellez_img_2740_original.jpeg)

The first dollar I had ever earned as a creative was way back in 2021. I was experimenting with creating watercolor paintings and was also freshly beginning the Spring semester at my university. I had connected with a friend of mine on campus who was also creating art at the time, and they had wanted to commission me for an original piece. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kevin-tellez)

## Kyle Medeiros![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778145862695-personal_1778145863620_1778145863620_kyle_medeiros_5.jpg)

The first dollar I ever earned from my creative work came from my book, Success is a Decision: The Success Story of Kyle Nunes Medeiros. Seeing that first Amazon sale notification felt surreal because it wasn’t just about the money — it was proof that something I created from my own experiences, mindset, and struggles had value to someone else. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kyle-medeiros)

## Sal Barracca![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778004222666-personal_1778004222220_1778004222220_sal_barracca_me.jpg)

In 1969, I graduated art school at the School of Visual Arts in NYC, majoring in illustration. After leaving the safety of art school it was now time to see if I could make it as an illustrator. I put together my portfolio of original pieces done in school and started calling art directors to show my work. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-sal-barracca)

## Chloe Marrazzo![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778178738001-personal_1778178737890_1778178737890_chloe_marrazzo_9263f9e7-922e-4014-a9b2-9a05f0f0774a.png)

The 80 Lanterns My first real ‘creative dollar’ came from a mix of persistence and a massive leap of faith. While working at the Commodore Perry Estate, I was constantly looking for ways to weave my art into the property’s atmosphere. I regularly shared my work and pitched ideas just waiting for the right opening. Eventually, the Creative director reached out about an opportunity . [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-chloe-marrazzo-2)
