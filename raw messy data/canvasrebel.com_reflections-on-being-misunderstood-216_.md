Often, those who tread off the beaten path are misunderstood or mischaracterized and so we asked folks from the community to reflect and tell us about the times they’ve been misunderstood or mischaracterized.

## Rainy Cates![Meet Rainy Cates](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778696244111-personal_1778696243445_1778696243445_rainy_cates_chatgpt-image-may-13-2026-02_16_56-pm.png)

I think one of the most misunderstood parts of my journey is that people often see me in pieces instead of as a whole. Some know me from entertainment and media, while others know me from corporate America and social impact work. What they don’t always realize is that both worlds are genuinely me. For a long time, I felt pressure to choose one. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-rainy-cates)

## Roksta![Meet Roksta](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778625815014-personal_1778625815231_1778625815231_roksta_15995020_10208683849315433_1057564560460061314_o.jpg)

This question.. there’s so much I can speak about upon this topic it’s unreal. Let’s start with 1 word. Censorship. This has become an important focal point for me because I never really realized how much I was being hidden from Earth’s population. I believe nobody really understands anything of how much of my music is me. I am music. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-roksta-2)

## CAJ![Meet CAJ](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778009642401-personal_1778009641890_1778009641890_caj_img_1617.jpeg)

I’ve always been passionate for all styles of music. I draw inspiration from everywhere, and also enjoy creating multiple styles. Often I used to be told I need to stick to one lane and one sound. Yet when I performed my live shows and went on this sonic journey of genres and soundscapes I found that the people enjoyed the entire experience. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-caj)

## Jim Harper

Here are some myths in my line of work that constantly haunt me. And I’m old enough to say all of these without fear of repercussion. 1.) I’m not sitting at my desk waiting for your call to make a change. I’m busy all of the time. You can’t get everything right back when you think of something. Sorry.
