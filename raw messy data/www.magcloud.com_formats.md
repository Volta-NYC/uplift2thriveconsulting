# MAGCLOUD PUBLISHING FORMATS

Whatever your project, we've got you covered

[Create](https://www.magcloud.com/create)

[![MagCloud standard magazine formats](https://www.magcloud.com/images/redesign/formats/Standard.jpg)](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/magazine)

Magazine


Good for: Magazines, Photo Books, Catalogs, Calendars


Available in 2 sizes, our Magazines can hold up to 384 pages, come in either Portrait or Landscape formats and are ideal for both personal and business projects.


[Create a magazine](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/magazine)

Pamphlet


Good for: Brochures, Real Estate Listings, Newsletters


Available in 4 trim sizes, Pamphlets are double-sided, 4-page publications, with a half-fold binding.


[Create a pamphlet](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/pamphlet)

[![MagCloud pamphlet formats](https://www.magcloud.com/images/redesign/formats/Pamphlets.jpg)](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/pamphlet)

[![MagCloud flyer formats](https://www.magcloud.com/images/redesign/formats/Flyers.jpg)](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/flyer)

Flyer


Good for: Handouts, Programs, Menus


Available in 5 trim sizes, Flyers are double-sided, single-sheet, with optional UV coating for added sheen and durability.


[Create a flyer](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/flyer)

Digest Publication


Good for: Portfolios, Look-Books, Mini-Catalogs, Booklets


Half the size of the Magazine, Digest publications can hold up to 384 pages and come in 2 trim sizes, Portrait or Landscape.


[Create a digest](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/digest)

[![MagCloud digest publication formats](https://www.magcloud.com/images/redesign/formats/Digest.jpg)](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/digest)

[![MagCloud square booklet formats](https://www.magcloud.com/images/redesign/formats/Square.jpg)](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/square)

Square Publication


Good for: Portfolios, Manuals, Photo Books


Compact and stylish, Square publications can hold up to 384 pages and come in 2 trim sizes.


[Create a square publication](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/square)

Tabloid


Good for: Portfolios, Cookbooks, Custom Calendars


Available in 3 trim sizes, Tabloid publications come standard with Wire-O Binding and are printed on premium cover stock with up to 90 pages.


[Create a tabloid](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/tabloid)

[![MagCloud tabloid paper formats](https://www.magcloud.com/images/redesign/formats/Tabloid.jpg)](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/tabloid)

[![MagCloud poster formats](https://www.magcloud.com/images/redesign/formats/Poster.jpg)](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/poster)

Poster


Good for: Events, Art, Photography


Full-color, single-sheet, and double-sided, Posters are printed on premium cover stock, come with UV coating.


[Create a poster](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/variations/poster)

Digital Options


Good for: Manuals, Magazines, Business Books


Whether free or paid, use our web-based viewer or downloadable PDF to sell and distribute a digital version of your project.


[Create a digital publication](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/digital)
