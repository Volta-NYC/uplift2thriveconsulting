Have you witnessed a time when you industry reversed course on something important? We’d love to hear about it, because when these U-Turns are covered up we lose out on our ability to better evaluate the current direction of our industries.

Frederick Reitz, Director of Security at Magnifica Air, approaches aviation security with the clear understanding that the industry is shaped—and reshaped—by defining “U-turn” moments. Events such as September 11 attacks fundamentally transformed aviation security overnight, shifting the paradigm from convenience-driven travel to a hardened, intelligence-led system built on prevention and layered defense.
