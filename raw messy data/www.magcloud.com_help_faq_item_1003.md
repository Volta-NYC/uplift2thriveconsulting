## What page sizes can I upload?

The PDF you upload to MagCloud should be one of the following sizes:

5.5" wide by 8.5" tall ( [Digest](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/digest), [Pamphlet](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/pamphlet) or [Flyer](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/flyer) products)

8.5" wide by 5.5" tall ( [Digest](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/digest), [Pamphlet](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/pamphlet) or [Flyer](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/flyer) products)

8.25" wide by 8.25" tall ( [Square](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/square), [Pamphlet](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/pamphlet) or [Flyer](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/flyer) products)

8.5" wide by 11" tall ( [Magazine](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/standard), [Pamphlet](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/pamphlet) or [Flyer](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/flyer) products)

11" wide by 8.5" tall ( [Magazine](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/standard) or [Flyer](https://www.magcloud.com/learn/products/flyer) products)

11.25" wide by 14.25" tall ( [Tabloid](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/tabloid) product)

14.25" wide by 11.25" tall ( [Tabloid](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/tabloid) product)

17.25" wide by 11.25" tall ( [Tabloid](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/tabloid) product)

12.25" wide by 12.25" tall ( [Square](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/square) product)

12.25" wide by 18.25" tall ( [Poster](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/poster) product)

18.25" wide by 12.25" tall ( [Poster](https://www.magcloud.com/products/formats/poster) product)

For more information about PDF specifications and step-by-step instructions to help you set up your file, visit our [PDF Design Resources](https://www.magcloud.com/create/resources) page in the Create section.
