Monochromica is the magazine for exceptional monochrome and color-dominant art. Each issue features juried art in the issue's monochrome color scheme. Issue 008's color is YELLOW/GOLD. Artist headshot, biography, statement, links, and artwork price are included. Curated artists are featured with a 2 page gatefold display with headshot, biography, artist statement, and, most important, their original art in full color. Social media and personal links for each artist are included.
