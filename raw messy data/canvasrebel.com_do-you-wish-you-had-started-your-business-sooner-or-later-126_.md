Almost every entrepreneur has had to decide whether to take the leap now or wait– perhaps they wanted to acquire more capital, experience or connections. Given how common this predicament is, we asked some successful business owners to reflect back on whether they wish they had started sooner or waited for a better time.

## Rickcale Peoples![Meet Rickcale Peoples](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779327604052-personal_1779327603947_1779327603947_rickcale_peoples_img_0987.jpeg)

If I could go back in time being a young single mother of 4 I definitely wish I would’ve started my business sooner. Before I started my journey of being a licensed manicurist and a realtor I was just “working” any job that will feed my family. I did not have any goals other than feeding my kids. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-rickcale-peoples)

## Skylar “SCKY REI” Reed![Meet Skylar “SCKY REI” Reed](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778904441218-personal_1778904441137_1778904441137_skylarsckyrei_reed_dsc06137.jpeg)

Well, I definitely wish I started sooner, I’ve always been a creative since grade school it’s how I made friends and I sold drawings of dragon ball z and Pokemon characters with the help my friends Devin Cook and Ike Westson (Musician) back as kids and got my first taste of entrepreneurship. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-skylar-scky-rei-reed)

## Kevin Gripper![Meet Kevin  Gripper](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779291923049-personal_1779291922696_1779291922696_kevin_gripper_facetune_02-05-2025-07-45-24.jpeg)

If I could go back in time I wish I would’ve started my creative career MUCH sooner. When I finally decided to pursue my creative career I was in my late 20’s, already a parent and really feeling the burden of working a 9-5 that I saw no real future for myself. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kevin-gripper)

## Kween Sankofa-Bey![Meet Kween Sankofa-Bey](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779055203647-personal_1779055202232_1779055202232_kween_sankofabey_signal-2025-11-08-19-18-30-133-42.png)

Looking back, I reflect sometimes on how far along I could be had I started my Fashion Design business sooner. I have always been a kreative. I have always loved the arts and throughout the years I have dabbled in writing, music and drawing. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kween-sankofa-bey)

## Skye Barker Maa![Meet Skye Barker Maa](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779156673122-personal_1779156672587_1779156672587_skye_barkermaa_sqh_skyejennwo_26_resized.jpg)

I will turn 53 this year, and becoming a fashion designer was never part of the plan—it was a complete surprise. I began designing just four years ago while trying to solve an unrelated business challenge and keep a team of skilled laborers employed. What started as a practical solution quickly became a calling. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-skye-barker-maa)

I spent a lot of my life thinking it would be impossible to be a musician in real life. I’m not sure why, my parents were very supportive of my and my siblings creativity. When I met Jason and we started writing songs together, everything changed. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-claire-molek)
