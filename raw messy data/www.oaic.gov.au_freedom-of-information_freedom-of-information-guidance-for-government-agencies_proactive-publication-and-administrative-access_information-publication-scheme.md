# Information Publication Scheme

## The Information Publication Scheme (IPS) encourages Australian Government agencies to release information to the public proactively. It is intended to encourage greater openness and transparency in government and reflects the pro-disclosure goals of the _Freedom of Information Act 1982_(FOI Act).
