# Privacy assessments

## The Australian Information Commissioner has powers to conduct [privacy assessments](https://www.oaic.gov.au/2022/privacy/privacy-legislation/the-privacy-act/privacy-assessment-powers) of agencies and organisations the _Privacy Act 1988_ covers.

The OAIC publishes finalised reports of these assessments (previously called audits) to help promote good privacy practice. If a report contains classified content, we may not be able to publish it.

Assessment and audit reports finalised since 2018 are listed below. Visit Trove for [older privacy assessments](https://webarchive.nla.gov.au/awa/20190509074057/https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy-law/assessments/Page-5) and [audits the former Office of the Privacy Commissioner conducted](https://webarchive.nla.gov.au/awa/20091112013024/http://www.privacy.gov.au/law/apply/audit).

See also [Consumer Data Right assessments](https://www.oaic.gov.au/2022/consumer-data-right/consumer-data-right-assessments)
