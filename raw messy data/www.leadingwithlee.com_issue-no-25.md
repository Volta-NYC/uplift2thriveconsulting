Anthony Craig is a fashionable micro-influencer whose passion for sneakers has evolved into a powerful story of art, culture, and self-expression. His love affair with sneakers began with the legendary Jordan 12 "Flu Game" and AirMax specifically the 95s, sparking a lifelong journey that would eventually grow into a collection of over 600 pairs of sneakers, including coveted releases from Nike, Jordan, Adidas, and more.

​

With a style that effortlessly blends fashion and footwear, Anthony’s outfits are always conversation starters. His knack for pairing statement sneakers with equally bold apparel has made him one of the go-to voices for sneaker culture and streetwear fashion. Beyond his style, his personality shines through, making him not only a collector but also a connector in the culture.

​

Anthony has collaborated with top brands like Nike and Champs Sports, bridging sneaker artistry with storytelling, while helping inspire a community of fashion lovers and sneaker enthusiasts who look to him for authentic insight and inspiration. For Anthony, sneakers aren’t just shoes, they’re Art, and what he likes to call the triple C’s… Culture, Creativity, and Confidence.

​

Most recently, Anthony was featured as the cover guy for Leading with Lee’s Fashion Meets Culture print issue, where his story was explored in depth. The feature dove into his passion for sneaker art, his journey as a style influencer, and how his collection reflects the intersection of fashion, identity, and culture.
