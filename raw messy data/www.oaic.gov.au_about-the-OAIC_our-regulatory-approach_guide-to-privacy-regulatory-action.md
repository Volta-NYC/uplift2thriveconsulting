Information:

The OAIC is updating this page to reflect changes to the [_Privacy Act 1988_ \- external site](https://www.legislation.gov.au/C2004A03712/latest/text) made by the [_Privacy and Other Legislation Amendment Act 2024_ \- external site](https://www.legislation.gov.au/C2024A00128/asmade/text) (POLA Act). These changes enhance the privacy of individuals with respect to their personal information, and include changes to the Information Commissioner’s enforcement powers. They also introduce a statutory tort to provide redress for serious invasions of privacy.

The POLA Act commenced on 10 December 2024, with some measures commencing at later dates as set out in s 2 of the Act.

For more information please refer to the Privacy Act and the [explanatory memoranda \- external site](https://www.aph.gov.au/Parliamentary_Business/Bills_LEGislation/Bills_Search_Results/Result?bId=r7249).
