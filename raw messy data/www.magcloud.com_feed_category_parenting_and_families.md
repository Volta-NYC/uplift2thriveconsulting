## [Family Court Evidence Organization Workbook](https://www.magcloud.com/browse/issue/3327714)

Feeling overwhelmed by screenshots, messages, emails, timelines, and paperwork?

The Evidence Organization Workbook was created to help individuals build a clearer, more manageable system for organizing records and documents.

Designed for people handling large amounts of communication records, exhibits, notes, and supporting documents, this workbook includes guided organization pages, printable logs, practical organizational tools to help simplify the process, and more!

Whether you are trying to sort through months or even years of records, this workbook is designed to help create structure, reduce overwhelm, and make information easier to review and manage.

Created by Kristie Gonzalez

For informational and organizational purposes only. Not legal advice.
