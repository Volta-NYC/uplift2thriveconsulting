# Australian Government Agencies Privacy Code

The Australian Government Agencies Privacy Code applies to all Australian Government agencies subject to the _Privacy Act 1988_ (except for Ministers). It requires all agencies to strive for best practice in privacy governance so they're all managing personal information to a consistent high standard.
