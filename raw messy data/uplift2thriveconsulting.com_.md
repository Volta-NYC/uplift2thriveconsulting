### About Chanté Ramsey

**Uplift 2 Thrive (U2T)** was born from a groundbreaking conversation that revealed a common gap for entrepreneurs: sales without clear brand storytelling, aligned mindset, and supportive systems.

Chanté is the driving force behind U2T, drawing upon **two decades of business infrastructure management, financial expertise, and problem-solving acumen**. She has been featured in **[Style N Soul Issue 23](https://www.magcloud.com/browse/issue/3267177)**, **[Humans of Fuzia](https://humansoffuzia.com/interview/chante-ramsey-helping-purpose-driven-businesses-grow-with-clarity-and-impact/)**, [**Canvas Rebel**](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-chante-ramsey/), [**Bold Journey**](https://boldjourney.com/news/meet-chante-ramsey/), and [**Leading With Lee Magazine**](https://www.leadingwithlee.com/chante-ramsey-2).

As a **seasoned brand strategist**, Chanté collaborates with businesses she holds dear, guiding entrepreneurs through growth while embodying a customer-centric perspective.

A resilient grassroots entrepreneur, **she navigated the challenges** with skillful pivots and maintained an unwavering can-do attitude. Through U2T, she eagerly channels her energy through the V.A.L.I.D. approach— **V** ISION, **A** CTION, **L** EADERSHIP, **I** NTERSECTION, **D** ETERMINATION— propelling businesses toward unprecedented success. **_Embrace your next chapter with Uplift 2 Thrive and redefine your brand’s triumphs!_**
