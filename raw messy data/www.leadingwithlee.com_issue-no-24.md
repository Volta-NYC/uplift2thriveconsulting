As an International Gospel Recording Artist, Singer/Songwriter, Celebrity Vocal Health Expert and Pedagogy Performance Coach; Mental Health Advocate, Speaker, and much more, Ashaala Shanae, is the Creative Director and Founder of The Music and Arts Healing Institute ( aka The M.A.H.I.) and LOVESTRUCK Inc., located in Miami, Florida. Ashaala has either vocally coached, produced, arranged, worked with, and/or traveled the world as a professional touring backing vocalist for celebrities, and recording artists in various genres, such as Gospel Music's, Yolanda Adams; Martha Munizzi; Jonathan Nelson; Joshua Rogers; Award Winning Artists such as Q Parker from Bad Boy's 90's RnB band, 112; and 2020 Grammy Nominated Recording Artist, lead singer of the New Orleans based group, Tank and The Bangas, - Tarriona "Tank" Ball. She wants to now show everyone in every industry the importance of finding your voice and using it to level up.
