Getting your first client is an exhilarating experience, but it’s also an experience that far too many aspiring entrepreneurs never experience. Sometimes, the difference between an idea dying in the idea stage or going on to change the world is getting that first customer – the first tiny win that creates the momentum to make an idea real. So, we connected with some of the smartest folks we could and asked them to tell us the stories of how they got their initial clients.

## Britt Sheflin  ![Meet Britt Sheflin](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777957338273-personal_1777957337763_1777957337763_britt_sheflin_britt0003.jpg)

My first client came to me during the end of my clinical internship. This client had a severe phobia of elevators and other enclosed and/or elevated spaces that was impacting her ability to function in day-to day life. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-britt-sheflin)

## Sandra Ortiz![Meet Sandra Ortiz](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778787830765-personal_1778787829946_1778787829946_sandra_ortiz_img_2137.jpg)

It was a truly special moment that marked the beginning of Rosemary and Brie. Having run my own cleaning business for nine years, I had built a wonderful, loyal community of about 250 clients. When I decided to transition into the world of charcuterie, I reached out to them to share my new passion. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-sandra-ortiz)

## Danielle Radin![Meet Danielle Radin](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778411038226-personal_1778411037676_1778411037676_danielle_radin_danielle_230628-76.jpg)

My story is unconventional, but the path I took is more accessible than people think. My first clients came after a video I posted on social media unexpectedly went viral. The video reached more than two million views, and in the weeks that followed, people from around the world began reaching out to me, asking for coaching and counseling. At first, I hesitated. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-danielle-radin-2)

## Richard Chisum ![Meet Richard Chisum](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778104851557-personal_1778104852737_1778104852737_richard_chisum_richard-chisum.jpg)

The Power of a Spec I got my first client (whom I had never met or spoken to) by walking unannounced into their place of business, carrying a portable cassette deck. The owner gave me a puzzled look and asked, ‘Can I help you?’ I set the cassette deck on the counter and pressed play. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-richard-chisum)

## Raven Finley-Flowers![Meet Raven Finley-Flowers](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778022666242-personal_1778022679473_1778022679473_raven_finleyflowers_raven-coaching-pic-resize.jpg)

My first dollar from the skills side of my volleyball business came in 2025—but the story really started long before that. I had spent years pouring into other programs, serving as a volleyball director with i9 Sports and coaching at the high school level. I was building athletes, leading teams, and gaining experience—but it wasn’t mine yet. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-raven-finley-flowers-2)

## Kim Ward![Meet Kim Ward](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776782129626-personal_1776782129110_1776782129110_kim_ward_img_7397.jpeg)

I had just completed my life & business coaching certification and the nerves were so strong but my determination was stronger. There was a woman that I was friends with on Facebook; she was hard working, dedicated and passionate but never seemed to stick with one thing. I knew that could potentially be an issue and maybe she couldn’t quite see it herself. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kim-ward-2)

## Elizabeth Ferris![Meet Elizabeth Ferris](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777831523515-personal_1777831523391_1777831523391_elizabeth_img_5522.jpeg)

My first non-friend or family photography job was actually a wedding, and it was my very first wedding ever—so it was kind of a double scary situation for me. I met her through my best friend, who also happened to be my boss at the time. She had originally asked him if he knew any photographers, and he told her about me.  [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-elizabeth-ferris)

## Divonya Battle![Meet Divonya Battle](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776797404268-personal_1776797403057_1776797403057_divonya_battle_img_0042.jpeg)

For two years, my dream of Phoenix Rising Home Care LLC sat on a shelf. I was working a stable night shift job, too terrified to step into the unknown. Then, the universe gave me a push: my job announced it was shutting down and laying everyone off. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-divonya-battle)

## Arshaad Norwood![Meet Arshaad Norwood](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777566351382-personal_1777566350965_1777566350965_arshaad_norwood_1-776a2410.jpg)

My first commission as a freelance artist outside of my foster home community was a family portrait for the dean of LGC. My sophomore year of college I took life drawing class that required weekly sketchbook assignments to develop hand eye coordination. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-arshaad-norwood)

## DESIREE’ SIMS![Meet DESIREE’ SIMS](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776890293800-personal_1776890293170_1776890293170_desiree_sims_me-and-mulberry-tree_105_c.jpeg)

I cried on the way to my first garden installation. I was so overcome with gratitude and just like pure joy… It was during the 2020 pandemic, my regular day job had become obsolete and here I was finally getting to be able to do what I wanted…helping people grow their own food at home. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-desiree-sims)

## Jaime Engebretson![Meet Jaime Engebretson](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777312033860-personal_1777312032925_1777312032925_jaime_engebretson_wedding-services-offered-by-minnesota-wedding-planning-company-jaime-events-full-service-to-day-of-coordination--scaled.jpg)

The journey to building my business started with my volunteering with my church to be their wedding coordinator and helping couples with their ceremony details. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jaime-engebretson-2)

## Dawn Hicks  ![Meet Dawn Hicks](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1775506018475-personal_1775506018079_1775506018079_dawn_hicks_headshot-1.jpg)

A friend of mine knew a BCBA (board certified behavior analyst) from her work and recommended I look him up and chat with him.
