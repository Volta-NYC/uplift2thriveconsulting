# Share Digital Publications Instantly

MagCloud is the first self-publishing service to give customers the power to distribute their content as free or paid publications in both print and digital formats. In addition to professional quality print products, you can make your content available via our web-based viewer, or as a downloadable PDF.

Just as with your print version, you set the price of your digital content. You can offer electronic magazines, books, business materials, and more for free or for $0.99 or more. You even have the option to bundle a free digital version with any print purchase. Check our [pricing calculator](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/pricingcalculator) for more information.
