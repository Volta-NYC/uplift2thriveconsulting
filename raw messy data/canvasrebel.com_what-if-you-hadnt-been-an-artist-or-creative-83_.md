Do you ever wonder what life would have been like had you chosen a more standard path? Does everyone have these doubts sometimes? We wanted to find out and so we asked as many talented artists and creatives as we could and have shared highlights below.

## Ytunde Oliver ![Meet Ytunde Oliver](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779258221259-personal_1779258219936_1779258219936_ytunde_oliver_picture.jpg)

I’m happy as an artist in the in-between stages. I can have the life of a bartender with my connections during and after work, inspiring me to build amazing experiences for people and provide a voice for artists. I see living my life as an artist is through curating experiences and building clientele at the bars. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-ytunde-oliver)

## Lauren Lewchuk![Meet Lauren Lewchuk](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779070908606-personal_1779070907846_1779070907846_lauren_lewchuk_lauren-lewchuk-resurgence2.jpg)

Being an artist has made me happier than I’ve ever been in my life. I’ve always been creative since I was a child. But the intentional choosing to be an artist full time has been the best decision I have ever made. It started off in an unfortunate situation being laid off during the pandemic. But I never looked back afterwards. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-lauren-lewchuk-2)

## Talia Lewis![Meet Talia Lewis](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779127921253-personal_1779127921144_1779127921144_talia_lewis_img_2680.jpeg)

Yes I’m happy, I like being able to wake up and get creative whenever! I like having free will, spending more time with my children. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-talia-lewis-2)

## Jade Patton![Meet Jade Patton](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779301930846-personal_1779301930581_1779301930581_jade_patton_kgz_0013.jpeg)

For me as an artist and a creative right now, I couldn’t be more happier because I feel like I have finally gotten where I would like to be from a place that started as a hopeful 19-year-old something girl, eventually acquiring a disposable job to save up for these endeavors, traveling to New York by myself with the money that I saved to visit an agency only to be told that I was too old at 23, [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jade-patton/)

## Amy Alley![Meet Amy Alley](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779111745182-personal_1779111745129_1779111745129_amy_alley_img_1050-1.jpg)

I am! I am an artist with a full-time day job, as most of us are. I needed the stability of a regular income when I found out I was having my son, whom I raised on my own and is a 21-year-old- university student at present. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-amy-alley)

## Robert Castillo![Meet Robert Castillo](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779548898697-personal_1779548898570_1779548898570_robert_blob-studio-instruments.jpg)

There are many times when I wonder what it’d be like to be ‘normal.’ By normal, I mean be okay with the mundane. Don’t get me wrong, I enjoy being abnormal, and by no means am I dismissing the mundane as an enjoyable aspect of life on its own right. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-robert-castillo-2)

## Adam Pecora

I worked in retail after of my post-college internship to support myself, and I delivered beer after COVID shut everything down. So, I spent about 7 years working “normal” jobs until I could get my foot in the door creatively. Suffice to say I do not miss it at all. I’m very grateful to be working in my field in any capacity. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-adam-pecora-2)

## Levi Ransom![Meet Levi Ransom](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779218447721-personal_1779218447638_1779218447638_levi_ransom_img_5010.jpeg)

I honestly don’t really know what I would be doing in my life other than this, a normal job sounds a bit scary to me, not in the sense that I don’t want to work, but in the sense that I have only known music as my main career for the longest time and don’t know what else I would be good at. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-levi-ransom)

## Lady Blanca  ![Meet Lady Blanca](https://canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779084685207-personal_1779084684897_1779084684897_lady_blanca_img_5067-copy.jpeg)

As a performer and creative, I am always met with different waves of creativity just as I am met with artistic block. Specifically as a drag performer who produces, hosts, performs, amongst other side endevours, it may seem as our creative flow is never-ending from an outside perspective. However, that is so far away from the truth. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-lady-blanca-2)

## Emily Davis

I have struck a balance in my life between my creative work and environmental science education/consulting that makes me very happy. My art and creative life is equally important to me as being a part of environmental projects that value improving the harmony between humans and the Earth. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-emily-davis-5)

## John Quinn![Meet John Quinn](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778939564504-personal_1778939564280_1778939564280_john_quinn_8e241e2a-655e-47e5-b57b-faca420ac449-41289-00000a0c74bda040.jpg)

I do have a regular job and I’m an animator. I moved to a town in my late twenties, after getting my degree in animation, where when you asked somebody ‘what they do’, they’d almost always clarify by asking ‘like for money or in my free time?’. That was great, I thought. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-john-quinn-2)
