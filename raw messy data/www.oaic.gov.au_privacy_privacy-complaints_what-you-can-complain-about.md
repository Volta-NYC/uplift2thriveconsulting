You can complain to us about the handling of your [personal information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/your-privacy-rights/your-personal-information/what-is-personal-information) by an Australian Government agency or any organisation the _[Privacy Act 1988 \- external site](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Series/C2004A03712)_ covers. The Privacy Act covers organisations with an annual turnover of more than $3 million, and [some other organisations](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/the-privacy-act/rights-and-responsibilities).

‘Handling your personal information’ means to [collect](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/your-privacy-rights/your-personal-information/collection-of-personal-information), [use or disclose](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/your-privacy-rights/your-personal-information/use-and-disclosure-of-personal-information) your personal information. The Privacy Act doesn’t apply to the personal information of a deceased individual.

Generally, a complaint must be about a matter that occurred less than 12 months ago.

## Your sensitive information

Personal information includes your [sensitive information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/your-privacy-rights/your-personal-information/what-is-personal-information#SensitiveInfo), for example:

- your [My Health Record](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/health-information/my-health-record/about-my-health-record)
- your [individual healthcare identifier](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/health-information/my-health-record/what-is-an-individual-healthcare-identifier)
- your genetic information
- your [credit report](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/credit-reporting/what-is-a-credit-report)
- your [tax file number](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/your-privacy-rights/your-personal-information/your-tax-file-number)
- the information the Medicare Benefits Scheme or the Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme holds about you
- your [criminal record](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/your-privacy-rights/criminal-records) (such as a spent conviction)
- information the [Personal Property Securities Register \- external site](https://www.ppsr.gov.au/) holds about you.

You can complain about the handling of your sensitive information.

## Before you complain to us

Contact the organisation or agency you think has mishandled your personal information to make a complaint. They should generally respond to your complaint in 30 days. If they don’t respond to your complaint, or you’re not satisfied with their response, you may [lodge a complaint](https://www.oaic.gov.au/_old/privacy/privacy-complaints/lodge-a-privacy-complaint-with-us) with us.
