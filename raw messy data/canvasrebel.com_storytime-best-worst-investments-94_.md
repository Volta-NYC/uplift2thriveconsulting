Risk is a topic that comes up a lot in our conversations with entrepreneurs and creatives, but a related topic is investments. Investments come in many forms – investing your time, money, heart and soul, etc. So, with that broad definition of investment in mind, we asked rising stars in the community to tell us the stories of the best or worst investments they’ve made.

## Tamryne Jefferson![Meet Tamryne Jefferson](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777089400562-personal_1777089400016_1777089400016_tamryne_jefferson_collage_export_03f7293b-acfb-439f-a2f8-6f4f678e2d00.jpeg)

Best investment I’ve made is the time I spent doing what I loved even though it meant wasting time on contracts that didn’t result in financial gain but were my stepping stools to the next stage in my career! When I first started getting contract work as a creative, I was screwed over each time. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tamryne-jefferson-2)

## Jacqueline Rios![Meet Jacqueline Rios](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776825899422-personal_1776825898836_1776825898836_jacqueline_rios_soirheadshots2026-7.jpg)

One of the worst investments I made as a business owner was hiring a Photography Business Coach at a time when I wasn’t fully ready to integrate what I was learning. At the time, I was highly motivated on a conscious level. I wanted to grow, refine my pricing and build a stronger client experience. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jacqueline-rios)

## Shavonne Bowman![Meet Shavonne Bowman](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776825801841-personal_1776825801359_1776825801359_shavonne_bowman_img_4467-1.jpg)

One of the worst investments I’ve made was putting about $90,000 into a trucking company. At the time, it looked like a solid opportunity. The industry was being promoted heavily, and it felt like a way to build something scalable. What I didn’t fully understand going in was how operationally intense it really was. I wasn’t just an owner, I became everything. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-shavonne-bowman)

## Jennifer Waithera Waweru![Meet Jennifer Waithera Waweru](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777421240858-personal_1777421240194_1777421240194_jennifer_waitherawaweru_jennifer-waithera-waweru-theatrical-headshot-5.jpg)

One thing I’ve tried to invest time in is trying to get to know myself outside of the surface level roles we all play in society. It’s taught me a lot about self perception, self compassion, remaining curious, and finding acceptance over those little quirks we all have and might feel embarrassed about but are actually the unique expressions of our identity as human beings. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jennifer-waithera-waweru)

## Melissa Matarazzo  ![Meet Melissa Matarazzo](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776972221420-personal_1776972220982_1776972220982_melissa_matarazzo_d1cbe286-0502-4132-83ca-44a38db1f7d8.jpeg)

The best investment I have made as a business owner, is investing in myself. I have expanded my skills to offer more complex jewelry creations, with materials that I dreamed of using, 7 years ago when I started. Serenity Threads’ original items started with hemp, cotton cording and glass beads. Now we offer stainless steel and 14k gold earrings, necklaces, bracelets and anklets! [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-melissa-matarazzo)

## Alison Gardner![Meet Alison Gardner](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777408902290-personal_1777408901801_1777408901801_alison_gardner_alison-gardner-founder-and-creative-director-at-perspektiiv-the-best-logo-and-brand-designer-in-colorado-and-pnw.jpg)

Hands down, it’s been my team. When I founded Perspektiiv in Colorado, I was doing what most creative founders do in the early days. Running every discovery call, designing every logo, writing every proposal, managing every project. It worked for a while. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-alison-gardner-2)

## Dr. Kimmery Newsom![Meet Dr. Kimmery Newsom](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776977238323-personal_1776977237855_1776977237855_drkimmery_newsom_img_0561.jpeg)

The best investment I made as a business owner was to bet on myself. I decided last July to enroll in a business consulting coaching academy called GetLaunched Academy with Dr. Erika Jordan-Thomas. I was nervous because I had not made this type of investment in my business, only in getting degrees. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-dr-kimmery-newsom)
