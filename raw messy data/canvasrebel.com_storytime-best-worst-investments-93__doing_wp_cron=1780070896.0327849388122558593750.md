Risk is a topic that comes up a lot in our conversations with entrepreneurs and creatives, but a related topic is investments. Investments come in many forms – investing your time, money, heart and soul, etc. So, with that broad definition of investment in mind, we asked rising stars in the community to tell us the stories of the best or worst investments they’ve made.

## Ashley Aleman![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774619088709-personal_1774619088236_1774619088236_ashley_aleman_pink-294-3.jpg)

The best investment I have made as a business owner is investing my time on building a brand I am passionate about. Building a brand is not easy as it takes months and years to get the results you want. I currently took a pause on my business due to personal reasons but breaks shouldn’t stop you from growing your brand. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-ashley-aleman-2)

## Izy![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1771071223713-personal_1771071223277_1771071223277_izy_img_5950.jpeg)

My greatest investment was myself. Investing in me mentally, physically, spiritually, emotionally, and energetically. Doing the internal work I needed to do to become the best version of myself. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-izy)

## Yvette Kelly-Fields![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1776300711600-personal_1776300711324_1776300711324_yvette_kellyfields_yvette-2024.jpg)

My best investment was an investment in coaching. I learned this as an executive director. When I started my business, I joined the coaching program ‘1Day1K’ led by Nicole Walters. Then, when I wanted to scale, I joined the Hello 7 coaching program led by Rachel Rodgers. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-yvette-kelly-fields)

## Niesja Strongwolf

It is incredibly important to take time to reflect on where you are going in life and to look inward to understand what is truly worth investing in. For me, the answer has been investing in myself. I have worked in real estate for many years, and along the way, I have experienced both the highs and the lows that come with building a career. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-niesja-strongwolf)

## Rachel Clem![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1776691682033-personal_1776691681530_1776691681530_rachel_clem_cas18026.jpg)

One of the best investments I’ve made in my business was purchasing a Simply Bread oven. When I first started, I was baking sourdough in a Dutch oven, which works beautifully when you’re making one or two loaves at a time. But as demand grew, that method just wasn’t sustainable. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-rachel-clem)
