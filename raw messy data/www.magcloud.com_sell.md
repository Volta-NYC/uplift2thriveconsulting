# Distribute & Sell Magazines

Get your content out into the world

[Get Started](https://www.magcloud.com/create)

MagCloud Storefront

When you upload a publication to MagCloud, you have the option to sell it online through the MagCloud storefront, adding a markup beyond the cost of printing to monetize your publishing efforts.


Readers can order your publication through the MagCloud storefront in print and digital format using a credit card or PayPal account. In addition, your MagCloud username doubles as a unique URL for your storefront at username.magcloud.com, making it easy to promote your publication online. You can also grant access to the storefront for private publications via a guest pass sent by email.


Shipping Options

Worldwide Shipping

MagCloud offers a range of shipping options to destinations around the world with delivery in as few as four business days. This worldwide reach means that even the smallest businesses can grow their audience to a global level. Got a client interested in seeing your catalog in the Cayman Islands? No problem. Need to send 50 copies of your brochure to Brazil? We’ve got you covered. Whether you're shipping portfolios to Portugal or albums to Australia, MagCloud ships orders of all sizes all over the world. Use our [pricing calculator](https://www.magcloud.com/formats/pricingcalculator) to find shipping options for your destination.


Direct Mail Services

MagCloud can manage your direct mail programs from start to finish, including custom printing, binding, personalized addressing, address cleansing and verification, and distribution to small or large groups.


Online Tools

Social Media

Sharing your new or favorite publications is as easy as a few clicks of the mouse. We've put all the promotional tools you could need at your fingertips to help you get the word out. Each publication's storefront page has a series of share links so you can promote your content on Facebook, Twitter, via email, or through a posted link on your blog or business site.


Launch Online Publications From Your Site

You can launch the web viewer version of your publication directly from your website or blog. From your publications's storefront page, simply select what you want your embeddable badge to look like, then copy the code provided into the site you wish to launch from.
If the publication is free, users can view it all, straight away. If you've set a price on your publication then they'll only be able to preview the first three pages and back cover and will be prompted to log in to access or purchase the full digital version.


Measure Success with the MagCloud Web Viewer Stats

Monitor how well your readers engage with your online publications via the Stats section in your account. You'll be able to see the number of total readers, page views, shares, hyperlinks clicked, and click-through rates for each publication.
