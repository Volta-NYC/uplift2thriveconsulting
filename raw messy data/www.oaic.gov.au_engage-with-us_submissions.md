# Submissions

## We engage regularly with the Australian Government and other organisations by providing privacy and access to information advice on a wide range of issues and proposals.

We’ll generally publish our formal submissions to government or government agencies in relation to policy proposals, discussion or issues papers, or exposure drafts of legislation.

Submissions to a parliamentary inquiry or committee will not be published prior to publication by the recipient. Submissions that include sensitive security information may be redacted or not published.

For older submissions, see [Trove](https://webarchive.nla.gov.au/awa/20170306173945/https://www.oaic.gov.au/engage-with-us/submissions/).
