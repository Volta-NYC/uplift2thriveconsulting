# Our FOI disclosure log

## Our FOI disclosure log lists information we’ve released under the [Freedom of Information Act 1982](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Series/C2004A02562) for public access.

The log has least two financial years of disclosure log entries. To view earlier entries see [Trove](https://webarchive.nla.gov.au/awa/20190509024000/https:/www.oaic.gov.au/about-us/access-our-information/foi-disclosure-log/). If you’re looking for information that isn’t on the log [contact us](https://www.oaic.gov.au/2022/about-the-OAIC/contact-us).

Documents are available to download in PDF format. If you’re unable to access this format, contact us and we’ll try to provide the document in an alternate format (for example, in Microsoft Word or large print hard copy). We don’t charge for providing access to information on our FOI disclosure log.
