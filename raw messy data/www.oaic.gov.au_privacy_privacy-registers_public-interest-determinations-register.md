This listing of current public interest determinations and temporary public interest determinations is the register of determinations that the Information Commissioner is required to keep under section 80E of the _Privacy Act 1988_.

For authoritative versions of all legislative instruments enabled by the Privacy Act see the [Federal Register of Legislation \- external site](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Series/C2004A03712/Enables). See also consultations for background on a [public interest determination](https://www.oaic.gov.au/engage-with-us/consultations/public-interest-determinations).

## 2025

Privacy (International Money Transfers) Generalising Determination 2025

**Name of determination**

[Privacy (International Money Transfers) Generalising Determination 2025](https://www.legislation.gov.au/F2025L00112/asmade/text)

**Start date**

12 February 2025

**Expires**

11 February 2035

**Other information**

This determination permits all authorised deposit-taking institutions within the meaning of the _Banking Act 1959_ to disclose the personal information of a beneficiary of an international money transfer to an overseas financial institution when processing an IMT without breaching the Australian Privacy Principles.

Privacy (International Money Transfers) Public Interest Determination 2025 (No. 1)

**Name of determination**

[Privacy (International Money Transfers) Public Interest Determination 2025 (No. 1)](https://www.legislation.gov.au/F2025L00113/asmade/text)

**Start date**

12 February 2025

**Expires**

11 February 2035

**Other information**

This determination permits Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Limited to disclose the personal information of a beneficiary of an international money transfer to an overseas financial institution when processing an IMT without breaching the Australian Privacy Principles.

Privacy (International Money Transfers) Public Interest Determination 2025 (No. 2)

## 2019

Privacy (Disclosure of Homicide Data) Public Interest Determination 2019

**Name of determination**

[Privacy (Disclosure of Homicide Data) Public Interest Determination 2019](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/F2019L00322)

**Start date**

20 March 2019

**Expires**

20 March 2026

**Other information**

This determination permits the Australian Federal Police to disclose certain personal information to the Australian Institute of Criminology for the purpose of the Australian Institute of Criminology's research under the National Homicide Monitoring Program and the publication of aggregate findings.

## 2018

Privacy (Australian Honours System) Public Interest Determination 2018

**Name of determination**

[Privacy (Australian Honours System) Public Interest Determination 2018](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/F2018L01418)

**Start date**

12 October 2018

**Expires**

12 October 2028

**Other information**

This determination permits the Department of Home Affairs to disclose personal information to the Office of the Official Secretary to the Governor-General and the Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet for the purpose of verifying the Australian citizenship and/or permanent residency status of individuals who are the subject of nominations for membership or honorary membership of the Order of Australia, or for other awards in the Australian honours system.
