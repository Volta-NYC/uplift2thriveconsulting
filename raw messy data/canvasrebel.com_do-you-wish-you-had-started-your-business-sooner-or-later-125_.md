Almost every entrepreneur has had to decide whether to take the leap now or wait– perhaps they wanted to acquire more capital, experience or connections. Given how common this predicament is, we asked some successful business owners to reflect back on whether they wish they had started sooner or waited for a better time.

## David Grabosky![Meet David Grabosky](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778639692308-personal_1778639688740_1778639688740_david_grabosky_1778268437756-1778268437113_david_grabosky_davidgrabosky_dec2024_web-2.jpg)

I think often how I had everything I needed to start my business when I did…and yet, that was also true at least two years or so before I launched. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-david-grabosky)

## Jimmy Viquez![Meet Jimmy Viquez](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777970140293-personal_1777970138207_1777970138207_jimmy_viquez_jimmy-viquez-canvasrebel-personal-photo.jpg)

Sooner, without question. Not necessarily in terms of going freelance earlier, but in terms of treating design like a business from the start. For a long time I was focused purely on the craft, and the business side of things, how to position myself, how to price, how to find the right clients, came much later than it should have. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jimmy-viquez-2)

## Milton Davis![Meet Milton Davis](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778672222366-personal_1778672222418_1778672222418_milton_davis_spacefunk-milton-2.jpg)

I’d always had a desire to write. However, I grew up in an environment where writing wasn’t a ‘real’ profession. So I pursued the sciences. I became a research chemist and created a successful career, but all the while becoming a writer stayed in my mind. I was 45 when I finally decided to make that dream a reality. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-milton-davis)

## Jackie Cetera![Meet Jackie Cetera](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777638566969-personal_1777638565851_1777638565851_jackie_cetera_jax-jax-.jpg)

If I could go back, I would have started my business sooner. I started JNC Innovations in June 2024, after working in higher education for 20 years. My work at colleges and universities was my life and I never saw myself leaving my career​. I measured success by working around the clock and the boxes ​I could check off in a 12 hour work day. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jackie-cetera)

## Kimberly Gonzalez![Meet Kimberly Gonzalez](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776900617387-personal_1776900616764_1776900616764_kimberly_gonzalez_2025-02_mmd-x-bake-up_merch-kitchen-shoot_007-2.png)

I am glad I started my business when I did. It’s easy to imagine that if I waited and had more experience or more training or more money that I could know what I know now and I could make better decisions or avoid any pitfalls or have more confidence, etc. etc.. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kimberly-gonzalez-2)

## Terra Blume![Meet Terra Blume](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777402493823-personal_1777402494392_1777402494392_terra_blume_img_0906.jpg)

I wish I would’ve started it way sooner. I had the idea for a long time to launch the business. I love wearing hats, and it was (and still is) a struggle to find hats that actually fit me, due to my hair. I knew if I started the hat business, I could also add on hats that fit big heads. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-terra-blume-2)

## Tiff Nutt![Meet Tiff Nutt](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1775834331155-personal_1775834330980_1775834330980_tiff_girls-trip-3473.jpg)

If I could go back, I would have started sooner but not in a rushed, regretful way. More in a “I wish I trusted myself earlier” kind of way. I officially started my business in 2017, while I was in college for graphic design. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tiff-nutt)

## Dylan Sherry![Meet Dylan Sherry](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778377794716-personal_1778377794338_1778377794338_dylan_sherry_img_5234.jpeg)

I mean there are always some things you wish you change I guess, but I try to not dwell on it. It would’ve been cool to be born a nepo-baby and never have to work a real job to supplement your dream. One thing I wish I could’ve done more was listen and not act like I had everything figured out when I clearly didn’t. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-dylan-sherry-2)

## Leopoldo Montemayor![Meet Leopoldo Montemayor](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777926079107-personal_1777926078592_1777926078592_leopoldo_montemayor_highfreqsession_camerachy6-01-2025_camerachy-12.jpg)

If I could go back in time, i would definately start making music sooner. DJing also. I started making music kind of late in life, after I started college, I was about 21 years old. Being in my first year of college, I really had no clue what I was going to do with my life. I just felt really restless, like something felt incomplete. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-leopoldo-montemayor)

## Mel McKenzie![Meet Mel McKenzie](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778444985372-personal_1778444978230_1778444978230_mel_mel-studio.jpg)

I feel like I was a late-bloomer as an artist. I have pockets of memories of drawing as a child, but never took it seriously and never opted for art subjects at school or university. I have zero regrets about that. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-mel-mckenzie)

## Amanda Fasciano![Meet Amanda Fasciano](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778106837095-personal_1778106840259_1778106840259_amanda_fasciano_1e6542ab-d449-4f9d-8d14-e3124ed4f278.png)

This is an interesting question because there’s so much I could speak to about it. I was an incredibly fortunate teen and when I queried Del Rey Books back in the late eighties, I received a request for a manuscript. Del Rey published all of my favorite fantasy authors at the time and I was ecstatic. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-amanda-fasciano)

## Peter Okonkwo![Meet Peter Okonkwo](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776966489984-personal_1776966488475_1776966488475_peter_okonkwo_1000462404.jpg)

I wish I had started my creative career sooner. I founded P. English Literature in 2020, and I really wish I had begun much earlier—back when a friend first recommended that I start a YouTube channel. It’s a shame that three years went by without me doing anything, but I’m still glad that I was able to start P. English Literature in 2020. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-peter-okonkwo)

## Natasha Mayet![Meet Natasha Mayet](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777157131535-personal_1777157130793_1777157130793_natasha_mayet_img_6403.jpg)

If I’m honest, for a long time I really believed I had started too late. I thought I was behind. I started acting at 29, after finishing my master’s degree, at a point where it felt like everyone else already had a head start. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-natasha-mayet-2)

## Tracey Lentfer![Meet Tracey Lentfer](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777264351178-personal_1777264350696_1777264350696_tracey_lentfer_screenshot-2025-02-28-at-92808-am.jpeg)

I graduated in 1983 with a degree in apparel design, did an internship in Lyon France for 9 Months, then Landed in Dallas working for dress companies. In the 80’s and 90’s was a great time for small apparel companies. I worked my way up to designer. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tracey-lentfer)

## Nicole Russell-Schlesinger![Meet Nicole Russell-Schlesinger](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776883232520-personal_1776883232043_1776883232043_nicole_russellschlesinger_studio1.jpg)

Growing up I always wanted to be an artist. It always felt like part of my identity. At some point in highschool, it was impressed upon me that it wasn’t the most financially stable or ‘wise’ choice so I reluctantly began to explore other options. I went to the University of South Carolina and majored in Early Childhood Education. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nicole-russell-schlesinger-2)
