Issue Two of Silly Fruit Magazine steps into the glitch. The moment when the system falters long enough for something honest to appear. Where the first issue planted seeds, this one wanders through the circuitry of our lives, asking what remains when the screens flicker and the endless scroll pauses long enough for us to notice ourselves again. Through fashion, art, drag, and storytelling, this issue explores how queer creators move through technology without letting it swallow them whole.
