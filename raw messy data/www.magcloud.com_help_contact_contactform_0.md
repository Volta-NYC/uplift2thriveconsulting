# Help:      Orders & Shipping

- [Ask MagCloud](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq "Ask MagCloud")
- [Contact Us](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact "Contact Us")


Before contacting us, check the [Ask MagCloud](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq) section for common questions we get asked. For information about creating a publication, please see our [Create](https://www.magcloud.com/create) section.


After contacting us, a member of our support team will respond via email within 24 hours, Monday through Friday 9am to 5pm PST.


- ## What can we help you with?

- I need to make changes to a recent order.I'm having trouble making a purchase.My order has not arrived.I received my order but there's a problem with it.I need help creating/uploading my PDF.I want a price quote.I'm a publisher and have a question about sales/payments.I want to print something MagCloud does not appear to support.I want to leave general feedback for MagCloud.None of the above.

- ![](https://www.magcloud.com/images/help/ContactUsCalloutArrow.png)


Cancel Your Order: Until your order has been sent to print, you can cancel it from your [Order History page](https://www.magcloud.com/account/orders). If you need to make changes to your file or the quantity of publications ordered, it’s best to cancel the existing order and then put in a replacement order.
