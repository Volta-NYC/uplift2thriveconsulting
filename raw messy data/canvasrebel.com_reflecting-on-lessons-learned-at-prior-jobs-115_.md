We asked some brilliant entrepreneurs, artists and creatives to reflect on lessons they’ve learned at prior jobs. We’ve shared highlights below.

## Nick Aliantro  ![Meet Nick Aliantro](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779225573610-personal_1779225573059_1779225573059_nick_aliantro_iconac-bio-pics-3.jpg)

Something important I learned in my first job that has stayed with me throughout my career would be how to be a productive employee underneath your manager even when you don’t have the answer. I was stressed the importance of having a problem solver mindset instead of a problem bringing mindset. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nick-aliantro)

## Diane Foster![Meet Diane Foster](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779129031951-personal_1779129031578_1779129031578_diane_foster_dwi00219.jpg)

I worked for many years as a computer systems analyst/programmer and, frankly, longed to be able to work on my painting for much of that time. But I appreciated the paycheck and other aspects of my job so I kept at it and worked in art when I could until I retired from IT at the end of 2019. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-diane-foster)

## Arielle Townsel![Meet Arielle Townsel](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778953143122-personal_1778953142968_1778953142968_arielle_townsel_img_0400.jpeg)

One of the most important lessons I learned before becoming a full-time business owner was understanding that professionalism, integrity, and relationships will take you further than talent alone. Before building Holly K. Co., I worked in creative spaces where I saw incredibly talented people miss opportunities simply because they lacked consistency, communication skills, or the ability to work well with others. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-arielle-townsel)

## Maddie Hagan![Meet Maddie Hagan](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779281284744-personal_1779281284611_1779281284610_maddie_hagan_img_2420.jpeg)

I worked as a licensed esthetician for 6 years before taking the leap to go out on my own and start my own business, Iris Skin Studio. During my time working for other people, I learned so many lessons that I now carry with me into every single treatment I provide. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-maddie-hagan)

## Miranda Beall![Meet Miranda Beall](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778870064061-personal_1778870064249_1778870064249_miranda_beall_2023_branding_miranda-3-1.jpg)

I’ve had so many jobs! [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-miranda-beall)
