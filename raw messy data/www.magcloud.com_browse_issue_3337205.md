MosFocus is a magazine that reveals the multifaceted world of modern life, where fashion, business, design, and art converge to foster inspiration and growth. Each issue highlights individuals who are transforming their surroundings, with authors exploring new trends, sharing insights, and uncovering unexpected aspects of the familiar.

In the latest issue, readers will discover Danatella's innovations in interior design, delve into Elena Loca's personal brand, and analyze market transformations in renovation with Alexander Nazarov. We also focus on design philosophy and the industry's future in discussions with Irina Leontyeva, while exploring the influence of emotional intelligence in business and the behind-the-scenes world of theater and cinema with Lana Krymova.

Projects like the Moscow-Vladivostok cycling trip encourage us to reassess the familiar, broadening our perspectives. Each article offers a fresh lens to view the world and find inspiration for personal achievements.
