Are artists born or made? To help answer this question, we asked some of the most artistic folks we know to tell us about how they knew they were going to pursue an artistic or creative path. We’ve shared highlights below.

## Seth Donahue![Meet Seth](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779250454816-personal_1779250455779_1779250455779_seth_donahue_img_6710.jpg)

I’ve ALWAYS known that I wanted to be in the field of entertainment. I grew up in the theatre community. I was the first Black boy accepted into Chicago’s Second City Summer Improv program. I’ve never been camera shy or had stage fright. I feel more comfortable on stage than I do in the real world. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-seth)

## Charles Breakfield![Meet Charles Breakfield](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779300042922-personal_1779300042561_1779300042561_charles_breakfield_aucruise_charles-ashography-web-3538.jpg)

I am drawn to solving puzzles. But puzzles began for me, building a book wholesale and retail business not completely understanding what had to be done next. From there it was the puzzle of earning a living to feed & house a family. That led to reinventing myself with a new career in computers. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-charles-breakfield)

## Des McAdam![Meet Des McAdam](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779067259089-personal_1779067258884_1779067258884_des_mcadam_2.jpeg)

I think I always knew. There was never really a version of me that didn’t want to create something beautiful and put it into the world. Even as a kid, I was drawn to anything artistic, fashion, photography, makeup, hair, modeling, storytelling. I loved transformation and self-expression in every form. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-des-mcadam-3)

## Jordan Groff![Meet Jordan Groff](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779203478031-personal_1779203477548_1779203477548_jordan_groff_img-20260518-wa1757.jpg)

The encouragement came from the beginning as a student in college. Initially it was just going to be a field of a study, but as I started to develop my skills, and my work was starting to be admired by my peers and professors, I knew there was potential for more. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jordan-groff)

## Raw J

I’d have to take everyone back to the early 2000’s as a kid. I grew up in the church so music’s been around me my whole life. My mom sings and she sang with some well known people, and my dad plays guitar. They had a group called the Mcnasty Band lol. Cut a deal and made a record called Save the children. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-raw-j)

## Darrell Shaw![Meet Darrell Shaw](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779141388622-personal_1779141388425_1779141388425_darrell_shaw_img-3191.jpg)

Life has never really been easy for me. I’ve always had to do things the hard way. Me and regular jobs just never really worked out lol. Crazy thing is, I’ve had a lot of jobs too, I just could never fully fit into that lifestyle. So I’ve always been a hustler, always trying to figure out different ways to create income and survive. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-darrell-shaw)

## Kevin Jackson II![Meet Kevin Jackson II](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779178155853-personal_1779178155651_1779178155651_kevin_jacksonii_img_4671.jpeg)

I became a Registered Nurse at the age of 19. To be transparent, growing up was complete survival mode. I never once thought of pursuing something outside of my comfort zone. Honestly, never even thought that to be possible. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kevin-jackson-ii)

## Peter Manning Robinson

I have been playing the piano since I was 3 years old. When I first sat down at the instrument, I said aloud, ‘I’ve done this before’ and was already playing my original music. I have never done anything else, nor would I want to. It is, for myself, the highest calling I could have. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-peter-manning-robinson)

## Joshua Williams![Meet Joshua Williams](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779210322810-personal_1779210322825_1779210322824_joshua_williams_ihwt31dc2.jpeg)

I had always been drawing since I was a child, but began to take it seriously at 12. I moved at that age and was in a moved to a more difficult school program than what I had previously. Because of these factors, art making become my way to communicate. It was essentially language. Throughout my career, I’ve gone through many phases of artistry. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-joshua-williams-5)

## Trinity Studios![Meet Trinity Studios](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778958935006-personal_1778958934910_1778958934909_adrian_alvarez_unnamed.jpg)

At Trinity Studios, we’re two best friends named Adrian and Jonathan who grew up sharing the same passion for animation, storytelling, and creating worlds through art. Starting this studio came from years of pushing each other creatively and realizing we wanted to build something of our own instead of waiting for opportunities to come to us. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-trinity-studios)

## Nayanika Vyas![Meet Nayanika Vyas](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779126098449-personal_1779126098204_1779126098204_nayanika_vyas_img_6226.jpg)

I think I always knew in some way, even before I had the language for it. Growing up in New Delhi, I was constantly drawn to the arts, and the dance studio became the place where I felt most like myself. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nayanika-vyas)

## EMMANUEL CHIBUIKE![Meet EMMANUEL CHIBUIKE](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779225665263-personal_1779225664214_1779225664207_emmanuel_chibuike_inshot_20260519_182008620.jpg)

Backstage at my high school talent show, I was shaking more from nerves than excitement. The curtain smelled like old velvet and dust, and I could hear the crowd buzzing on the other side. I had one number to do, but honestly, I was watching everyone else more than focusing on myself. On stage were seniors who moved like they owned the air. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-emmanuel-chibuike-2)

## Zhizhong Chen![Meet Zhizhong Chen](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779170917156-personal_1779170916973_1779170916973_zhizhong_chen_410dbca7dd15564d80844dcfb541460e.jpg)

I think I first realized I wanted to pursue something related to art or creativity when I was still in elementary school. At the time, I already had a very clear dream of getting into an art academy someday. During class, I would often secretly draw underneath my textbooks instead of paying attention. For me, drawing never felt like a task or an assignment. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-zhizhong-chen)

## Beau Yotty![Meet Beau Yotty](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779225381501-personal_1779225381678_1779225381678_beau_yotty_beau-yotty.png)

I think I always had a pull toward storytelling, history, and the opportunity to elicit an emotional response from written words. Larger than life circumstances, that bring out the hero in all of us. The moment it became real for me was when I realized I didn’t just enjoy creating, I couldn’t stop thinking about it. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-beau-yotty)

## Marine Zhou![Meet Marine Zhou](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778263277392-personal_1778263277260_1778263277260_marine_zhou_c3eeea38-4ef6-42ea-9026-66d4240aa06d.jpg)

Before I came to AFI, I studied International Communication. In that major, I learned a lot about culture, society, and media, and I became really interested in how stories and images can shape the way people think. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-marine-zhou)

## PJ Peterson  ![Meet PJ Peterson](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779314156948-personal_1779314156404_1779314156404_pj_peterson_4a44ff0c-205c-43bf-82ab-ba9c435a6a4e_1_105_c.jpg)

Years ago when I was flying home from my first vacation in the  , I started writing about it in my journal. I’d had such a wonderful time that I filled several pages. The next thing I knew, I started imagining scenes that eventually became a full-length book! [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-pj-peterson-3)

## Briana Johnson  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778812115389-personal_1778812115305_1778812115305_briana_johnson_beautiful-the-artist-headshot-683x1024.png)

I was about 10/11 years old when I first knew I wanted to pursue a creative/artistic path for my life professionally after giving my life to Christ. I was born and raised in Atlanta, GA. I am the only one in my family who is Deaf. I grew up around a musical environment. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-briana-johnson-2)

## Jennifer Nangle  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779043998709-personal_1779043998692_1779043998692_jennifer_nangle_mlb_01-685x1024.jpg)

I loved to draw and color as a child, but when it came to sports, I never had that inkling. When I was ten years old, I went to see a staged play of ‘The Sound of Music’ where family friends played the von Trapp children. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jennifer-6)

## Dutch Hippies![Meet Dutch Hippies](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779224791466-personal_1779224791263_1779224791263_dutchhippies_photo-output.jpeg)

Eva and I met in 1998, when she was 15 years old and signed with Sony Music Entertainment in the Netherlands. At the time, I was performing with my own band and writing original songs. One day, I heard Eva sing at a jam session, and instantly I had a vision of creating a band with her. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-dutch-hippies-2)

## Andrea Morgan![Meet Andrea Morgan](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779206471245-personal_1779206470914_1779206470914_andrea_morgan_andrea-morgan_headshot.jpg)

Like every single artist on the planet, I’ve always been a creative. I didn’t give myself ‘permission’ to fully explore painting (buying supplies, wasting paint, making mistakes, taking some courses) until after my kids moved out and I found myself with extra space for a studio and some extra time for me. Once I started, I could not stop. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-andrea-morgan)

## Desmond Noland![Meet Desmond Noland](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779064132720-personal_1779064132584_1779064132584_desmond_noland_3bc153f3-06bd-4f7e-90b6-d0ee3313dc70.jpeg)

2014 is the first time I wanted to take art serious. Quick backstory, I’ve always loved art as a kid. I knew since about the age of 5 that art spoke to me. Fast forward, right after high school me and my homie Sean started painting on clothes and just overall being creative. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-desmond-noland)

## Ronnie Sanders![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779072308478-1779072323616_ronnie_sanders_e3-kozco.jpg)

Several years ago, I wanted to start a new, community orchestra in 2017. I decided to hold auditions and thought that if we had fifteen or twenty maybe thirty players, we could find some violins, a couple of flutes, maybe some trombones, maybe a trumpet or two and be able to have a, you know, a nice little community orchestra. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-ronnie-sanders)
