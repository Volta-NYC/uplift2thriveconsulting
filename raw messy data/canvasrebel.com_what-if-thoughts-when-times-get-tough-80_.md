When times are good it’s easy to feel like entrepreneurship is the only path for you, but what about the times when your hit with multiple crises at once? We wondered if all entrepreneurs have moments when they wonder whether they should have gone with (or stuck to) a more standard job where they worked for someone else.

## Alma Vicencio![Meet Alma Vicencio](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779330424871-personal_1779330424675_1779330424675_alma_vicencio_img_9626.jpeg)

Thinking about a regular job makes me want to vomit. I’ve worked a traditional job before. I know what it’s like to build someone else’s vision, to clock in and out, to leave your full self at the door. Never again. Now I’m the owner of Alma Styles and I wake up every day learning, growing, and using my talents in fashion. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-alma-vicencio)

### Kevin Lewis

I would say I enjoy where I am right now, doing both. You don’t get to hear many people who say that they really enjoy their “regular” job and their business ventures. From the business venture side and KF22, I get to build and create something that’ll outlast me far after I leave this planet. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kevin-lewis-3)

## Kelly Thomas![Meet Kelly Thomas](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778958037257-personal_1778958036598_1778958036598_kelly_thomas_2024-photo-shoot-38.jpg)

There are definitely moments when I think about what it would be like to have a regular job again. I think every business owner has those moments, especially when you carry not only the weight of the business, but also the responsibility of the people who depend on you. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kelly-thomas-4)

## Mel Bell![Meet Mel Bell](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778937216591-personal_1778937216472_1778937216472_mel_bell_mel-grit-11.jpg)

Happier? Yes. But happiness isn’t the word I’d use. Built for it is closer. The irony is, I never saw myself as a business owner (let alone, a two-time business owner). I always thought, ‘That could never be me. I’m too risk averse.’ But then I became one and realized my entire adult life had been quietly disagreeing with me. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-mel-bell)

## Katie Meckstroth![Meet Katie Meckstroth](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779292480555-personal_1779292482147_1779292482147_katie_meckstroth_0.jpg)
