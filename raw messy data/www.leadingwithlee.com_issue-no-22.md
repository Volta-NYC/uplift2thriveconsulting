#Girlboss and Entrepreneur Latoya Nicole cover "The Entrepreneur Issue" of Leading with Lee! Latoya Nicole is a bestselling author, publisher, and social entrepreneur. She is from North Carolina and has received a Master's Degree in Counseling/Psychology. She is creating her own lane with her brand Entrepreneurs Color Too while bringing diversity to adult coloring books that are aimed to help women relieve stress. Her books have been featured in Curly Nikki, Black Enterprise, Madame Noire, Official Black Wall Street, O, The Oprah Magazine, and more. Being passionate about uplifting females in the community she also teaches emerging entrepreneurs how to start and market their online businesses.
