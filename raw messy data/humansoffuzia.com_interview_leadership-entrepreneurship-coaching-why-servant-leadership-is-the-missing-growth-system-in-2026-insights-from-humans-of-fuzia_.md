# Leadership, Entrepreneurship & Coaching: Why Servant Leadership Is the Missing Growth System in 2026 — Insights from Humans of Fuzia

![](https://humansoffuzia.com/wp-content/webp-express/webp-images/uploads/2026/04/Scott.png.webp)

#### Scott Doggett

In a world where 69% of employees feel disengaged, the real leadership crisis of 2026 isn’t talent—it’s trust.

At Humans of Fuzia (HOF), where leadership, entrepreneurship, coaching, and women empowerment intersect, one truth consistently emerges: businesses don’t fail due to lack of strategy—they fail due to broken cultures. And few stories illustrate this better than that of leadership expert Scott Doggett.

## From Corporate Leadership to Purpose-Driven Entrepreneurship

After 30 years in corporate hospitality, Doggett’s career took an unexpected turn. A post-COVID layoff pushed him into reflection—and eventually into purpose.

“I thought that was where God had brought me to stay,” he shares about his role at a global nonprofit. But a second layoff shifted his perspective again.

What followed became the foundation of his entrepreneurial mission.

At the nonprofit, Doggett witnessed servant leadership at scale—an organizational culture so strong that employee turnover dropped to single digits.

“It was night and day… nobody ever wanted to leave this beautiful environment.”

That moment reframed everything: leadership isn’t about authority—it’s about how people feel under your leadership.

## The 2026 Leadership Challenge: Culture vs. Cost

Doggett’s journey highlights a key tension modern entrepreneurs face:

- Businesses need growth
- But budgets are tighter than ever
- And culture investments are often the first to be cut

“Training, development, recognition—all of that got stripped away,” he explains.

Even today, many organizations haven’t rebuilt those systems.

The result? High disengagement, low retention, and stalled growth.

## The Visibility vs. Conversion Gap in Coaching Businesses

Despite strong audience resonance, Doggett faces a common entrepreneurial challenge:

“I’ve gotten in front of big audiences… everybody agrees this is critically important… but how do we convert that into actual business?”

This is a familiar gap for coaches and leadership entrepreneurs:

- Strong message
- High audience interest
- Low conversion into paying clients

The insight here is clear: **agreement does not equal commitment**.

## Actionable Growth Systems for Coaches & Entrepreneurs

Doggett’s approach offers practical strategies for scaling impact:

### 1\. Authority Through Speaking

He’s prioritizing conferences, podcasts, and public speaking—building trust before selling.

### 2\. Scalable Expertise Model

Instead of doing everything solo, he built a faculty of 20+ experts—creating leverage without full-time hiring.

### 3\. Smart Outsourcing Over Hiring

“I would rather outsource… and reinvest in the people that I have.”

This reflects a modern growth system:

- Stay lean
- Scale through partnerships
- Avoid premature hiring

### 4\. Purpose-Led Leadership

“For me, it’s about following your calling… I wake up every day excited.”

In a noisy market, clarity of mission becomes a competitive advantage.

## Execution Tip

**Audit one leadership decision this week:**

Before making it, ask— _“Does this prioritize people or just performance?”_

Then choose the version that strengthens trust.

## Leadership Insight: The Future Is Human-Centric

As AI reshapes workplaces, fear around job security is rising. Doggett believes this makes servant leadership not optional—but essential.

“We need that style of leadership more than ever.”

The future of leadership isn’t more automation—it’s deeper human connection.

## Conclusion: Building Businesses That People Don’t Want to Leave

Doggett’s journey is a powerful reminder: sustainable growth isn’t built on tactics alone—it’s built on culture.

For entrepreneurs, coaches, and leaders navigating 2026:

- Invest in people-first systems
- Build authority through visibility
- Focus on mission over short-term uncertainty

Through platforms like Humans of Fuzia, these real, unfiltered leadership stories are shaping a new era of conscious entrepreneurship—where growth and impact go hand in hand.
