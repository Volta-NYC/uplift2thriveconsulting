Starting a business is hard because it’s a fight with yourself, an internal battle – gaining the courage to get started, etc. Scaling a business is different – the challenges you face are mostly external to yourself. Each challenge has a million mini-challenges. We wanted to create a space for conversations and stories around growth and scaling. Below, you’ll find stories and insights from successful entrepreneurs across a variety of industries and markets.

## Liv Longin![Meet Liv Longin](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779069998031-personal_1779069997754_1779069997754_liv_longin_img_7957.png)

I think one of the biggest misconceptions about business growth is that people assume you start with a perfectly mapped out scaling plan. I really didn’t. When I started \[Bozeman Media Design\](https://www.bozemanmediadesign.com?utm\_source=chatgpt.com), I just recognized a genuine gap in the market. ## Jay Ternavan  ![Meet Jay](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779164051378-personal_1779164045609_1779164045609_jay_ternavan_jay-ternavan.jpg)

One thing I’ve learned is that businesses rarely grow in a straight line. From the outside, people sometimes assume that if a company becomes successful, there must have been some big breakthrough moment where everything suddenly took off. In reality, scaling JayWay Travel was much slower and far more relationship-driven than that. When I first started, the business was incredibly small and very hands-on. ## Brianna Acevedo![Meet Brianna Acevedo](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778884766116-personal_1778884765916_1778884765916_brianna_acevedo_kino-greece.jpg)

Hello, my name is Brianna Acevedo and I’ve been running my small dog training business out of my home for over 10 years. I started very small, in a typical neighborhood home and would offer training solutions to members of the community. Synonymous to running my business I’ve always been extremely passionate about furthering my education through accomplishing goals with my own personal dogs.
