Wanting to be an artist or creative is step one. Following through means investing in learning your craft, but we’ve heard from so many aspiring artists and creatives who feel unsure about where to start and so we’re incredibly grateful to the artists and creatives who’ve agreed to share their stories of how they learned their craft.

Music Learning Summary How music is really learned Most producers learn through repetition, copying, experimentation, and finishing projects. Recreating songs helps train: arrangement rhythm sound selection transitions emotional flow Over time, producers stop hearing songs casually and start hearing: frequencies stereo space compression texture energy movement Fastest ways to improve 1. Recreate music you love Rebuild drums, melodies, effects, and structure. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-lillowe-williams)

## Chris Cuellar![Meet Chris Cuellar](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779332841183-personal_1779332841002_1779332841002_chris_cuellar_photo-2.jpg)

I started learning how to play guitar at a young age. My inspiration came from rock stars such as Slash, Buckethead, and Synyster Gates from Avenged Sevenfold. I taught myself by learning songs from these artists, which, at the time, took forever to learn because I was so inexperienced with the instrument. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-chris-cuellar)

## Jorge Cancino

I learned by doing the job. In this line of work, I feel that having that rapport with clients is very important, if not the most important thing. Photographers and videographers are among the few vendors who are with the couple almost all day, and it’s important to have that connection with them. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jorge-cancino)

## Autumn Havlik![Meet Autumn Havlik](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779168577766-personal_1779168576744_1779168576744_autumn_havlik_autumn_havlik.jpg)

If I am being completely honest, I learned to act throught the art of play. Growing up, I had a vivid imagination and, getting lost in worlds of my own, I played an aray of characters. I think that this desire for play and an outlet for my imagination never really left me, just grew into a more refined craft. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-autumn-havlik)

## Crystal Wreden![Meet Crystal Wreden](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779165809247-personal_1779165809060_1779165809060_crystal_wreden_me-with-rig-painting.png)

My main artistic influences have been my mother, who is a formally trained painter, as well as other artists in my extended family. I grew up surrounded by art on the walls and my parents often took me to museums and exhibitions, usually at the High Museum of Art in Atlanta, Georgia. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-crystal-wreden)

## Nelle Gallub![Meet Nelle Gallub](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779324197997-personal_1779324197941_1779324197941_nelle_gallub_nelle_portrait1.jpg)

I got a very early start as an artist. My mom painstakingly saved a myraid of drawings I did as a kid, and I can honestly say that I never drew a stick figure in my life. My underdeveloped child-brain actually had some phantom understanding of form and volume. As I grew older, I found myself drawing my favorite cartoon and video game characters. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nelle-gallub)

## Helena Tueffel![Meet Helena Tueffel](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778939954456-personal_1778939953730_1778939953730_helena_tueffel_1000020703.jpg)

I did a lot of drawing as a child. I also liked to make beaded jewelry. In Wisconsin. I had art classes in elementary school but it was my high school art teacher who really got me excited about art. My college didn’t offer an art degree but I continued to draw and paint as I got my BA in Journalism and Communications. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-helena-tueffel)

## Matt Jones![Meet Matt Jones](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779052451213-personal_1779052451083_1779052451083_matt_jones_img_8310-copy.jpeg)

How did you learn to do what you do? Music has always deeply moved me. Since I was a little kid, I’ve listened both out of sheer enjoyment, and to process the intense emotions I’ve always felt. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-matt-jones-3)

## Linda Sue Price![Meet Linda Sue Price](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779238509734-personal_1779238509522_1779238509522_lindasue_price_lsp-in-studio.jpg)

I have been bending neon tubes for over twenty years. I also worked in video production for years producing cable programs and creating motion graphics. Learning to bend neon tubes was and is harder than learning new software—at least for me. And I am still learning… It requires focus, patience, practice and each time I pick up a tube it is a risk. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-linda-sue-price-2)

## E Edwards![Meet E Edwards](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779204659375-personal_1779204660528_1779204660528_e_edwards_breon-osaka-castle-17.jpg)

I started by doing a ton of research before I even bought a camera. I walked into my local camera store and spent almost an hour talking with one of the sales reps. I didn’t buy anything that day. Instead, I went home, watched countless videos on the basics and the art of photography, and eventually decided to go back for my first purchase. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-e-edwards)

## Jeremy (Nvest) Navarrette![Meet Jeremy (Nvest)](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779240993258-personal_1779240992861_1779240992861_jeremynvest_navarrette_img_9474.jpeg)

It all started with a love of drawing as a kid, then graffiti caught my attention in college, where I met writers who opened my eyes to a whole subculture. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jeremy-nvest)

## Hee Tak Jung![Meet Hee Tak Jung](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779171981112-personal_1779171981004_1779171981004_heetak_jung_photo.jpg)

One of the biggest shifts in my career came from realizing that design is not only about creating visually exciting concepts, but about understanding how those ideas are actually brought into reality. Early on, I was very focused on the creative side of interior design and architecture, especially concept development and visualization. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-hee-tak-jung)

## Jennifer Y![Meet Jennifer Y](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779364167384-personal_1779364167185_1779364167185_jennifer_img_1743.jpeg)

Learning the craft has all been a self taught journey. I’ve had nobody to ask or a teacher to guide me so it has all been a learning process by making mistakes and learning from them. I am still learning and I feel as though you can always keep learning and perfecting your craft. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jennifer-y)

## M. Yurevych

The World is Ambigrammatic, threaded with love and all of its faults; because of this we have to search for the hidden places on this earth to make an understanding of it. Man trying to understand the wisdom of earth throughout the centuries lead me on a quest of understanding symbols, and craving perspective that could have been lost or overlooked. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-m-yurevych)

## Don Miller![Meet Don Miller](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778951798455-personal_1778951798356_1778951798356_don_miller_20240521_143949.jpg)

I went back to school later in life after becoming disabled due to an injury that made me endure two spinal fusions, and I still have problems with my spine. I only picked up metalsmithing around two years ago during my BFA, where I took some metals and jewelry classes taught by Mindy Herrin-Lewis, and really enjoyed working with metals. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-don-miller)

## Sarah Kuehn![Meet Sarah Kuehn](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779156372378-personal_1779156372301_1779156372301_sarah_kuehn_facetune_23-06-2025-10-37-01.jpeg)

I actually taught myself how to make jewelry and continue to do so to this day! It all started when I wanted to make myself some statement pieces using my favorite raw gemstones. So I got a few books and started there. Every day after work and every weekend, I would experiment and try things until I eventually got good at it. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-sarah-kuehn)

## Rodney Roldan![Meet Rodney Roldan](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778928327780-personal_1778928327984_1778928327984_rodney_roldan_primary-image.jpeg)

I learned by doing and honestly, by being thrown into situations where I had no choice but to adapt quickly. ## Boris Amado![Meet Boris Amado](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778589609293-personal_1778589609144_1778589609144_boris_amado_img_3176.jpeg)

So I basically taught myself how to write screenplays. I knew that I wanted to write something with a narrative, so I went and looked up the screenplays for, Full Metal Jacket and Goodfellas. I also looked up the screenplay for the Wizard of Oz. And that’s how I taught myself to write. I also took a class from Pilar Alessandra to perfect my craft. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-boris-amado-4)

## Seema Kasthuri![Meet Seema Kasthuri](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779161383731-personal_1779161383412_1779161383412_seema_kasthuri_58127.jpg)

From a very young age I started learning Indian classical music. I only have my parents to thank for. They knew this was my talent when I was really small, as young as 4 or 5 As a typical teenager I must say I took this craft a little too lightly. It was just my inherited talent that always let me shine. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-seema-kasthuri)

## Manny Gonzalez![Meet Manny Gonzalez](https://canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779134076667-personal_1779134076506_1779134076506_manny_gonzalez_img_0634.jpg)

I first started learning about this craft when I was gifted a camera at 14 years old. ## Mya Valentine![Meet Mya Valentine](https://canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778816294912-personal_1778816294860_1778816294860_mya_valentine_dsc_6759-copy.jpeg)

## Kevin Charlesv

Didn’t learn my craft through a traditional path, I built it in real time, under pressure. My foundation started in hands-on environments, working as a technician where precision, repetition, and discipline weren’t optional, they were survival. That experience taught me how to execute. But execution alone isn’t enough, you have to think bigger. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kevin-charles)
