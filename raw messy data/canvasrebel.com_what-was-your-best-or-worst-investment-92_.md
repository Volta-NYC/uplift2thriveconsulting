There is so much to learn from investments that went really well as well as those that went really poorly and so we asked some of the wisest creatives and entrepreneurs in the community to tell us the stories of their best and worst investments.

## Aza Goodwin  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/c-1773167842863-personal_1773167842323_1773167842323_aza_goodwin_img_1575.jpg)

One of the best investments I’ve made in my career was attending Coco Rocha’s modeling camp. At the time, I had already been modeling for a while, but I wanted to refine my skills and better understand the professional side of the industry. When I applied, I was surprised at how quickly they responded, which pushed me to seriously consider the opportunity. [Read more>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-aza-goodwin)

## Regina Sage  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/c-1772822070610-personal_1772822072355_1772822072355_regina_sage_final-logos-for-regina-01.png)

The best investment you can make for a creative career—or really, any career—is in yourself. It might sound cliche, but learning, growing and exploring things that interest you not only enrich your life but they inform your art in unexpected ways. [Read more>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-regina-sage)

## Regina Sih-Meynier  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/c-1773098629318-personal_1773098628881_1773098628881_regina_sihmeynier_reginas-headshot_vertical-hand-to-neck.jpg)

The best investment we made as self-employed solopreneurs was purchasing my husband’s family home in the French countryside during the pandemic. Through what felt like divine intervention, a random—but perhaps not so random—taxi driver told my brother-in-law that his former father-in-law’s estate, which had been sold more than 30 years earlier, was back on the market. [Read more>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-regina-sih-meynier)

## Madison Clark  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/c-1772929227691-personal_1772929227284_1772929227284_madison_clark_img_6520.jpeg)

My best investment is and will always be my future. You have to put in the work and invest in yourself so it pays off later. Put all your time and energy into what you want for your life. Put your heart, your soul, your money, your time, your every thing into it. I did that with many, many things. [Read more>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-madison-clark-2)
