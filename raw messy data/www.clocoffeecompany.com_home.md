### We have curated the best coffee beans from all over the world to serve New Jersey

> We are looking forward to serving and cultivating the **true coffee experience** in New Jersey. Coffee lovers in our area have sacrificed quality for convenience for way too long. At CLO, we are focused on our roasters working with farmers that use best practices to ensure we serve the highest quality, eco-friendly, shade-grown coffee beans. Coffee beans cultivated in high altitude to generate rich flavor in every cup.
>
> Have your taste buds experience a world tour of flavorful beans from our favorite regions like Ethiopia, the place many consider the birthplace of coffee. No matter the region, from crop to cup, our roasters maintain the coffee beans quality from inception to indulgence. Get the **true coffee experience** by exploring what CLO has to offer, and taste what happens when quality comes first.

BE KIND

We want to put a spotlight on kindness. If there’s one thing we want you to leave with aside from a great cup of coffee, it’s that CLO is a place where kindness is the most important ingredient in our coffee recipes.

![IMG_2840.jpg](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5e08007c6d6c7a1fb0aa58c8/1618521443790-YL23H9QXEHY2GF8LNDG8/IMG_2840.jpg)

Take Action

Doing good for others feels good, and small gestures can go a long way. Today, try to smile at a stranger, pay for someone's coffee, or do other random act of kindness. Our spark of hope for our coffee lovers around the world is to spread kindness. Coffee and kindness are similar in many ways. Cultivation of both involves dedication and effort. CLO coffee is like kindness - it awakens your spirit, increases your happiness, and improves your day. So, let us fill your cup with both!
