Are artists born or made? To help answer this question, we asked some of the most artistic folks we know to tell us about how they knew they were going to pursue an artistic or creative path. We’ve shared highlights below.

## Bryan Hammond

## ![Meet Bryan Hammond](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778569779953-personal_1778569779850_1778569779850_bryan_hammond_img_2259.jpeg)

Wow! It seems like so long ago, well! My first time realizing that I had a creative ability inside of me, was when I wrote my first song.. Now, mind you I was in elementary school, so I must have been about 8 or 9 years old. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-bryan-hammond)

## Michelle Khonsari![Meet Michelle Khonsari](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778641376518-personal_1778641377029_1778641377029_michelle_khonsari_me_bookshelf_ravencrest.jpg)

The first time I knew that I wanted to pursue a creative path professionally, I was five years old. I have loved books for quite possibly my entire life. I loved to be read to, and by the time I was just two or three years old, I wanted to read them myself. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-michelle-khonsari)

## Shioban Ashford![Meet Shioban Ashford](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778783333540-personal_1778783333438_1778783333438_shioban_ashford_copy_866a6b2c-2f8f-4a90-9f50-58563619f7b4-1.jpeg)

At a very young age. When I was young around 3 or 4 and I wasn’t attending school. My mom would get my siblings on the bus and start her cleaning. We had the entertainment system that had A TV, a record player and storage included. She would sit me up there and put on a record and sing and dance with me [. Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-shioban-ashford)

## Garet Hanson![Meet Garet Hanson](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778173248448-personal_1778173247630_1778173247630_garet_hanson_img_7634.jpg)

Growing up in Willows, CA, a small rural town where agriculture basically was the economy, the career path was pretty clear for most people: own land, be a farmer, make it. I knew from a young age that wasn’t my calling. What I did know was that I loved making things. Art projects, painting, anything hands-on were always my favorite parts of school. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-garet-hanson)

## Amanda Vidican![Meet Amanda Vidican](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778023640623-personal_1778023640155_1778023640155_amanda_vidican_img_9187.jpeg)

Ever since I was a child, I loved to draw. It was a very calming experience. My parents divorced at a very young age. So I think I took to art as a type of escapism that still applies today. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-amanda-vidican)

## Alex Emelina![Meet Alex](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778610449919-personal_1778610450553_1778610450553_alex_emelina_men1lji_.jpg)

I knew I wanted to pursue an artistic path professionally when I was 16. I fell in love with all things art including painting and designing. Project Runway was a major reason I wanted to get into the fashion world, but it wasn’t until I met a student in college that told me she was a Theater Major, that I became inspired to pursue acting. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-alex-6)

## Olivia Mertin![Meet Olivia Mertin](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777870076613-personal_1777870076025_1777870076025_olivia_mertin_040526_jax-6.jpg)

I can’t remember ever having a different answer to the question of what I wanted to do when I grew up. The words formed so instinctively on my tongue: I want to sing. I am not sure when exactly this idea originated within me. Perhaps the long car rides with my dad and sisters, singing along to Pentatonix, trying to nail their harmonies. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-olivia-mertin)

## Wayne Flint![Meet Wayne Flint](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778498426187-personal_1778498426297_1778498426297_wayne_flint_7-669a332c0c19d_blackdigit__dgb-lol-sharp-6x.jpg)

I don’t think there was a single moment where I suddenly decided to become an artist—it was more of a gradual realization that I kept returning to it no matter what else I was doing. I remember spending a lot of time experimenting creatively, just trying to understand what I could build visually and how far I could push ideas digitally. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-wayne-flint-5)

## Tasha Parks![Meet Tasha Parks](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778129922169-personal_1778129921779_1778129921779_tasha_parks_img_9855.jpeg)

My foundation has always been rooted in the arts. For as long as I can remember, I’ve been deeply creative – it was never something I had to learn, but something that came naturally to me from a young age. I grew up immersed in it all – choir, dance, theatre, cheer, drill team. Creativity was always a constant in my life. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tasha-parks)

[Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-amaris-dwarkah)

## Christen Reed![Meet Christen Reed](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778606907544-personal_1778606907289_1778606907289_christen_reed_img_8137.jpeg)

## Karen Ferguson  ![Meet Karen Ferguson](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778607456622-personal_1778607456520_1778607456520_karen_ferguson_img_3103-2.jpeg)

## James Matthews![Meet James Matthews](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778511512233-personal_1778511512037_1778511512036_james_matthews_1000002775.jpg)

## Gwen Kowalski![Meet Gwen Kowalski](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778425529422-personal_1778425529303_1778425529303_gwen_kowalski_img_5831.jpeg)

We sold our house in October of 2020 and moved to downtown Orlando, embracing a more active urban lifestyle. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-gwen-kowalski)

## Jennifer Way![Meet Jennifer Way](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778608274200-personal_1778608273838_1778608273838_jennifer_way_img_8849-2.jpg)

## Nicole St. Laurent![Meet Nicole St. Laurent](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777139979318-personal_1777139978045_1777139978045_nicole_stlaurent_459481637_10236956364468704_15970380372457630_n.jpg)
