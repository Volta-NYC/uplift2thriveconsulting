Working on something meaningful is a common desire – but how? We started asking folks to share the story of how they ended up working on a project they felt was meaningful because we wanted inspiration but also because we wondered if hearing from people from across a range of industries would help us identify patterns making it more likely for anyone to be able to find and work on projects that they would find meaningful.

## Andy Sorenson![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778727421673-personal_1778727421450_1778727421450_andy_sorenson_andy-sorenson-2.jpg)

The most meaningful projects I’ve been a part of are the two that I am currently working on. Composing music under the name Aumen for meditation, breathwork, yoga, somatic practices and more…and an indie-folk duo project called Borrowed Wings which focuses on vulnerable and honest lyrics and songwriting. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-andy-sorenson)

## Inha Dybchenko![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778612297254-personal_1778612297056_1778612297056_inha_dybchenko_photo_2026-01-29_11-03-38-3.jpg)

The most significant project I have ever had the opportunity to work on was my solo exhibition, ‘Inside Motherhood: From Expecting to Becoming’. Held in Sacramento in January 2026, it marked a pivotal moment in articulating my artistic philosophy. The subject of motherhood is often discussed through the prism of lofty ideals, burdensome expectations, and glossy, idealized imagery. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-inha-dybchenko)

## Milli Oshyun![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778431495239-personal_1778431494854_1778431494854_milli_oshyun_profile.jpg)

The most meaningful project I have ever worked on is my recently released first full-length album, \[Based From Sonny\]. I initially started my musical journey with the goal of becoming a K-pop composer. As I continued to learn and produce, I was exposed to a vast array of music. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-milli-oshyun)

## Adam Frost![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778609467428-personal_1778609467179_1778609467179_adam_frost_highland-park-guitar-lessons-adam-frost-guitar-lessons-dallas.jpg)

One of the most meaningful projects I have worked on was recording my own original music with Jack Endino. Jack is a Seattle producer, engineer, mixer, and musician best known for his work with the early grunge scene. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-adam-frost)

## CAITLIN TRAINER![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778518234970-personal_1778518234831_1778518234831_caitlin_trainer_trainerfamily325.jpg)

If I’m being completely honest, I don’t think there’s one specific project that stands out. When I hear the word “project,” my mind immediately goes to a single session, but the most meaningful work I’ve done has really been creating art that reflects a family’s real season of life. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-caitlin-trainer)

## Josh Rivers![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778705894951-personal_1778705894687_1778705894687_josh_rivers_dsc09349.jpeg)

Creating the immersive theme events is very meaningful to me. It gives a reason for my family and friends to gather. It provides a safe space for everyone to express themselves, I love seeing the creativity everyone puts into their looks! We spend about six months preparing for a party, so it’s wonderful enjoying time with my friends crafting and creating, brainstorming, trial and error. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-josh-rivers-2)

## Iryna Babych![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778557065331-personal_1778557065096_1778557065096_iryna_babych_ib2-2.jpg)

To be honest, every project I work on is meaningful to me, because photography has never been just a profession for me—it has always been something deeply personal. I truly love what I do, and every story I capture becomes part of my journey as an artist. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-iryna-babych-2)

## Sedona Regan![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778702639290-personal_1778702639128_1778702639128_sedona_regan_dsc02021.jpeg)

The most meaningful project I have ever worked on is actually the album that I am currently developing! This project has been a long time coming and feels like a culmination of all the years I put in to hone my craft [. Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-sedona-regan)

## Mysti Cozart Smith![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778607709129-personal_1778607708586_1778607708586_mysti_cozartsmith_screenshot-2025-12-13-220142.png)

Love Comes Home To Holly-A Romantic Holiday Film-is probably one of the most impactful and meaningful projects I have ever created. This story is about finding faith, family and realizing that the heart knows when it’s time to come home. I wrote the script for this movie around 2016/2017 after I had met my now husband Brad. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-mysti-cozart-smith)

## Colleen Herriges  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778690069848-personal_1778690069406_1778690069406_colleen_herriges_img_2636.jpg)

I feel like it is pretty safe to say that all of my art comes form a meaningful place. I didn’t start REALLY exploring art until about 3 years ago after leaving a horrifically absuive marriage and landing in a new city. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-colleen-herriges)

## Bill Page![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778198859643-personal_1778198859569_1778198859569_bill_page_bpage.png)

I think just being an artist is a meaningful life-long project, a complex life consuming project. And once you enter into the realm of being an artist it consumes your entire being and you just can’t decide to stop. Everything you do, how you look at everything, everywhere you look, it’s related to who you are as an artist. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-bill-page)

## Dr. Lashonda Wofford![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778109842454-personal_1778109841721_1778109841721_drlashonda_wofford_b413a31c-a7e2-4a0c-8974-48bd459f5d5b.png)

One of the most meaningful projects I’ve ever worked on is being the Visionary Author of Breaking the Chains: Liberating Your Lineage from Generational Curses, From Brokenness to Brilliance: Finding Purpose and Passion in Your Pain Volumes I and II, and authoring Seasons of Assignment: When Purpose Shifts and Destiny Still Calls while leading the transformational movement behind these life-changing projects. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-dr-lashonda-wofford-2)

## Yueqi (Vicky) Zhang![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778720908085-personal_1778720907974_1778720907974_yueqivicky_zhang_image.jpg)

One direction I’d love to explore is how remote and hybrid work have permanently changed the expectations people have for their personal work environments. As a Senior UX/Product Designer and Design Lead focused on connected workspace software experiences, a large part of my work involves designing systems that help monitors, peripherals, docks, audio devices, and workspace tools work more seamlessly together. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-yueqi-vicky-zhang)

## Laura Romanov![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778178819564-personal_1778178819003_1778178819003_laura_romanov_img_7533.jpeg)

I think we never truly know which moment in our lives will become the beginning of something much bigger. For me, it started with a small bird my son and I tried to save. The experience deeply changed both of us. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-laura-romanov)

## Stacie Frazier![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778163730726-personal_1778163730283_1778163730283_stacie_frazier_img_7182.jpeg)

Honestly, the most meaningful project I’ve worked on is “Let Me Talk My Ish: The E Ness Documentary.” When I first met with E Ness at the very start of this project, he opened up to me about something that really hit home. He didn’t feel like he had the love and respect of his peers in the industry. That conversation changed everything for me. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-stacie-frazier)

## Lorelai Paige![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778166836772-personal_1778166837083_1778166837083_lorelai_paige_c0d8aebc-e607-4e82-a8be-353aeaf0dcb3-1_all_20192.jpg)

The most meaningful project I’ve worked on would be my most recent album, ‘Chaotic Brilliance’. I fully indulged myself into exploring pop and alternative rock sounds. It allowed me to express different parts of myself I never really put focus on. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-lorelai-paige)

## Jeannie Seidel![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778173889632-personal_1778173880767_1778173880767_jeannie_seidel_jeannie-seidel.png)

The wands I create have many layers of symbolism and meaning that are derived from my own life experiences growing up as a female in a patriarchal society. I explore themes related to feminism, the invisible labor of women in society, and goddess / witch archetypes. My assemblage journey began in 2009 when I inherited a box of my grandmother’s vintage jewelry. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jeannie-seidel)

## Michael Hofstadter![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778509185989-personal_1778509185817_1778509185817_michael_hofstadter_rebel-photo-1.jpeg)

My most meaningful project is the novel that I’m currently working on. It’s a spiritual sci-fi thriller that involves reincarnation and how things in this lifetime or the last, will never truly be forgotten. There are a couple of reasons why this project has been significant to me. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-michael-hofstadter)

## Hailey Yoon![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779039920763-personal_1779039920473_1779039920473_hailey_hy.png)

One of the most meaningful projects I’ve worked on was leading the rebranding of Nicka K New York, a beauty brand with over 30 years of history in New York’s multicultural beauty market. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-hailey-yoon)

## Lia Baek![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777978875437-personal_1777978872917_1777978872917_lia_baek_img_3948.jpg)

One of the most meaningful projects in my career was working on the global campaign for Decision to Leave and Broker at the Cannes Film Festival. It was a particularly significant moment because it was the first time a single Korean investment and distribution company, CJ ENM, had two films invited to compete in the main competition in the same year. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-lia-baek)

## Kevin DelMastro![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778003814346-personal_1778003813859_1778003813859_kevin_delmastro_0141_251109_niwa_107a3341.jpg)

Performing sax at an Indian wedding recently was quite meaningful. First, there were 1,000+ people and several key coordinators, the MC and DJ. The couple had dreamed up this idea to use sax to complement the music, even though there are other traditional instruments. After success in planning it, I was struck by the energy, the rhythms, the colors and the dances. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kevin-delmastro-2)

## Nita Chawla![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778177479129-personal_1778177478997_1778177478997_nita_chawla_img_5054.jpg)

One of the most meaningful projects that I’ve worked on is my song ‘The Way’. I wrote this song in 2013 actually after a conversation with my sister and her friend (both of whom are writers/playwrights). Her friend asked me if I had ever written about something personal, something that tells my story. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nita-chawla)

## Rachel Ha-Eun Lee

In recent years, it has become essential to me that the work I create or am part of, specifically dance-wise, feels like it is making a statement about something, and connects to those who may not have a dance background. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-rachel-ha-eun-lee)

## Stephanie Pierce![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778065425769-personal_1778065425332_1778065425332_stephanie_pierce_img_4019.jpeg)

The most meaningful project I’ve worked on is creating The Story Room, a creative studio built around connection, storytelling, and community. The idea came from a gap I kept seeing in both my work as a therapist and a photographer. People want spaces where they feel safe to show up as themselves, but many environments feel transactional or surface-level. I wanted to create something different. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-stephanie-pierce-2)

## Kristen West![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777948271990-personal_1777948271520_1777948271520_kristen_west_img_5813.jpeg)

The most meaningful projects I have worked on have been for my own family. I was able to illustrate both of my children’s engagements and special things for their weddings like invitations and signage. I had the chance to give my parents an illustration of my childhood home where they lived for over 50 years [. Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kristen-west)

## Taylor Cohen-Caravella![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778641872033-personal_1778641871953_1778641871953_taylor_cohencaravella_screenshot-2025-05-20-at-21832-pm.png)

I started something called “Creative Concepts” where it is a discounted shoot because the person who books and I are making art in real time with a concept I come up with. I have had so much fun challenging myself in these concepts whether it is involved around a mirror, red lighting, a vail, etc. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-taylor-cohen-caravella)

## Casey Gu![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777851769527-personal_1777851769102_1777851769102_casey_gu_head-shot_xinyue-gu_16x9.png)

The most meaningful project I’ve worked on is the Maxon One Brand Expression Film. As the sole motion designer at Maxon, I was tasked with communicating an entire product ecosystem to a global audience. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-casey-gu)

## Mae Cody![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777681330119-personal_1777681329684_1777681329684_mae_cody_img_3426.jpeg)

The most meaningful work I’ve done isn’t just one project—it’s turning my own survival into something that helps others find theirs. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-mae-cody)

## Emiko Kawakubo![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777905302329-personal_1777905301642_1777905301642_emiko_kawakubo_emiko-kawakubo.jpg)

The most meaningful project I’ve worked on so far has been designing social media visuals for the concert series of The Unprecedented Music Association-UMA-. As a relatively new designer, every project feels like a challenge, but this one stood out for its concept—an exploration of fusion across the five senses. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-emiko-kawakubo)

## Joshua Burton![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778306122813-personal_1778306122388_1778306122388_joshua_burton_img_6785.jpeg)

One of the most meaningful projects I’ve worked on is the creation of my poetry film ‘Iridescence’. After winning a contest, I was paired with a local filmmaker in Houston to create a poetry film. I wrote a new poem for the film and me and the filmmaker collaborated on how to best bring the film to life. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-joshua-burton)
