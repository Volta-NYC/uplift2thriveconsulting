# Privacy legislation

The _Privacy Act 1988_ applies to Australian Government agencies and organisations with an annual turnover of more than $3 million, and some other organisations.

Most state and territories also have their own privacy laws which cover their public sector agencies.

And there are a number of Australian laws other the Privacy Act that relate to privacy.
