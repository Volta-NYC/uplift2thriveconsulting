New England has had its tales of ghostly encounters since before we became a nation. However, do we really understand what we are seeing and what we hear? As an investigator, I can say you need to go into an investigation as a skeptic and keep your mind open to whether you believe a place is haunted or not.

I have seen many things that I cannot explain from dark shadowy figures to the ghost of a French soldier. I am part of a team called Haunted in New England and I also do freelance investigations.
