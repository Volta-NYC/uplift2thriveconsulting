Every once in a while we have the good fortune of working on a project that we feel truly matters, a project that we’ll still be thinking about years from now. Maybe even something we can imagine telling our grandkids about – surely you’ve had moments like that where something you did in your professional life really mattered?

## David Reminick![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778559240290-personal_1778559240154_1778559240154_david_reminick_rem260313_112f.jpg)

The most meaningful project I’ve worked on has to be the album I’m currently releasing: Fun Intended – my first full album of children’s music. For years, I had considered writing children’s music, but only began seriously after becoming a parent. Initially, I made the songs very simple because I assumed that was what kids needed. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-david-reminick)

## Agie Gee

One of the most meaningful projects I’ve worked on has been building and evolving my creative world through DJ Agie Gee and what later expanded into projects like Red Renaissance and The Public Experiment. What started as simply a love for music and creative expression slowly became something much deeper, a mission centered around creating intentional experiences that genuinely move people emotionally, mentally, and spiritually. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-agie-gee)

## RB Vigneshwar![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778641947854-personal_1778641947924_1778641947924_rb_vigneshwar_vigneshwar_photo.jpg)

The most meaningful project I have worked on is a TV pilot titled Paper Dreams. It is a crime thriller drama that follows a single mother and former counterfeiter who, drowning in debt and living out of her car with her teenage son, returns to forgery to provide for him. It began with a photograph. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-rb-vigneshwar)

## Komathy Caandeepan![Meet Komathy Caandeepan](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777762923282-personal_1777762921511_1777762921511_komathy_caandeepan_ga300808.jpg)

Throughout my teaching career, one of the most significant challenges I’ve encountered is the ‘diversity gap’ in classroom literature. Too often, my students struggled to find stories that resonated with their lived experiences. This lack of representation served as a call to action for me; I began writing to create the inclusive narratives I knew my students were missing. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-komathy-caandeepan)

## Pratima Aravabhoomi![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777852299297-personal_1777852298631_1777852298631_pratima_aravabhoomi_studio-shot-aravabhoomi-cropped.jpg)

One of the most meaningful projects I’ve worked on was a commissioned piece for a client who asked me to respond to a memory she couldn’t photograph or really capture in a direct way. It wasn’t tied to a specific image or single moment from her childhood, it was more about a feeling she carried. Something fragmented, emotional, and hard to fully describe. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-pratima-aravabhoomi)

## Diane Fleisch Hughes![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777831936241-personal_1777831935657_1777831935657_diane_fleischhughes_img_7550.jpeg)

For years, I’ve facilitated twice-monthly life drawing sessions, open to any artist who wants to participate. I started them because there was a need in my area—attending a life drawing session meant a long drive, and I wanted to build something local and accessible. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-diane-fleisch-hughes)

## Melissa Dumas![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778446483317-personal_1778446483126_1778446483126_melissa_dumas_me.jpeg)

The meaningful project I’ve worked on was an Independent film called ‘Fried Chicken Day’ Its a feel good coming of age film written and directed by my longtime friend, Alan Smith. The film is shot in my hometown (also where Alan is from), and in our highschool that we graduated from. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-melissa-dumas)

## N’kole Bryant![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778719936201-personal_1778719935570_1778719935570_nkole_bryant_1000035490.jpg)

One of the most meaningful projects I have worked on was \*Courageous Convos\*, featuring the legendary civil rights activist Ernest McMillian. The event was a powerful panel discussion that included seven panelists alongside Mr. McMillian, centered on the current state of our community and how we can collectively work toward meaningful solutions and healing. Mr. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nkole-bryant-2)

## Joquina Reed![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778282603651-personal_1778282602944_1778282602944_joquina_reed_0472026.png)

One of the most meaningful projects I’m working on is the one I’m building right now: The 250 Project: A Black and Indigenous Reckoning. The project began as a response to the upcoming 250th anniversary of the United States in 2026. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-joquina-reed-2)

## Natalie Warren![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777877502096-personal_1777877499631_1777877499631_natalie_warren_natalie-warren_photo.jpeg)

There are ongoing projects, and one of my most meaningful projects has been creating a workshop celebrating the 200-year legacy of Louis Braille and Music Braille through Braille Institute. Louis Braille was the French educator who invented the raised-dot reading system used by blind and partially sighted readers around the world. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-natalie-warren)

## John Randall Nelson![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777910688858-personal_1777910688380_1777910688380_johnrandall_nelson_01-nelson.png)

One of the most meaningful projects I’ve worked on splits between a public piece and a body of studio work—they feed each other. On the public side, ‘Tree of Life’, at Cortez Park in Phoenix, stands out. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-john-randall-nelson)

## Drew Stevens and Jace Allen N/A![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778120642838-personal_1778120642288_1778120642288_drewstevensandjaceallen_na_paint-splodge-insta-post-2.jpg)

The most meaningful project for me personally has been our most recent release, “In a Tree.” It’s a love song I wrote for my partner, Kate, and every step of the creative process felt right “in the pocket,” to borrow some musical lingo. I wrote the song in our bathroom (where the reverb is just right) and felt like I was really onto something. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-drew-stevens-and-jace-allen)

## Rebecca Swan![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778109572635-personal_1778109572070_1778109572070_rebecca_swan_img_1290.jpg)

To date the most meaningful project I have worked on was a production of Romeo & Juliet in which our main characters were both women who were grappling with a community that did not support queer love and had drawn political lines in the streets. This felt particularly important because so many areas in this country are divided in the exact same way. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-rebecca-swan)

## Tara Dodds![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778093547952-personal_1778093547175_1778093547175_tara_dodds_dsc_6317.jpeg)

One of the most meaningful projects I have worked on was with a fellow creative where I did my first video and acting work. I shared a song that had significance in my life, and he knows the story of what I have been through in the past few years. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tara-dodds)

## PJ Turner![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778679399772-personal_1778679401314_1778679401314_pj_turner_526158341_3970937843156838_8151823009344940983_n-2.jpg)

As a team of artists and cannabis enthusiasts, we have a series of projects called Flighted. Each edition is released on 4/20 of every year, of course. While these albums are themed to fit the vibe of the stoner, we were careful to put some meaningful, soul-nourishing, reflective, and movement invoking tracks in to diversify the project and the series. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-pj-turner)

## Darnell McKenzie  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777953286267-personal_1777953284998_1777953284998_darnell_mckenzie_imdbprocard.jpg)

When I think about the most meaningful project I’ve worked on, it’s the cinematic universe I’m currently building through my company, Visual Dynasty Productions, LLC. I call it meaningful because it goes beyond entertainment—it’s rooted in purpose. Everything I’m creating is designed to come full circle with my faith, ultimately pointing people toward the Kingdom of God. [Read more>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-darnell-mckenzie-2)

## Tricia Copeland  ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777979845267-personal_1777979845213_1777979845213_tricia_copeland_tricia-copeland-author-photo.jpg)

In 2020, as many did, I felt isolated, especially from my creative tribe. I worked and home-schooled with my husband and three teenage children, all right-brained humans, and even with a massive gardening project, I craved interaction with other authors. Enter my Toastmasters group and a project that entailed starting a podcast. My left-brain cheered. [Read more>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tricia-copeland)

## Patricia Araujo![Meet Patricia Araujo](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777525151238-personal_1777525150679_1777525150679_patricia_araujo_patricia-painting-a-mural-of-pacifica-at-arttitud-2015.jpg)

I’ve had several memorable projects I’ve been a part of and one that has stayed with me through the years has been painting (in oils on canvas) the statue of Pacifica and forgotten monuments of the Golden Gate International Exposition, also known as the GGIE– the last world’s fair of San Francisco Bay! [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-patricia-araujo)

## Ori Peer![Meet Ori Peer](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777419950170-personal_1777419949068_1777419949068_ori_peer_1.png)

The most meaningful project I’ve worked on is “THIS FILM WAS MADE WITHOUT AI” – a public domain community resource I organized to mark and celebrate projects which are Al free. In December 2025, I launched an open call for artists to create their own labels reinterpreting a simple sketch: two green angels saying ‘THIS FILM WAS MADE WITHOUT AI. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-ori-peer)

## Karla Ebrahimi-Camens![Meet Karla Ebrahimi-Camens](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776982117585-personal_1776982116982_1776982116982_karla_ebrahimicamens_karla-skyofblue-james_wirth_photography_sob-102823_dsc5745-.jpg)

I am a graphic designer and many years ago I was working long hours creating corporate graphics when I decided I couldn’t stomach churning out another design piece that would eventually make its way to the recycle bin anymore. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-karla-ebrahimi-camens)

## Stephanie M. Wytovich![Meet Stephanie M. Wytovich](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776787741708-personal_1776787741826_1776787741826_stephaniem_wytovich_wytovich_stephanie_pic.jpg)

I wrote my collection, On the Subject of Blackberries, three months postpartum, in a fever-dream state, using bibliomancy as my main guide. It was vital in helping me process my PPD and recent OCD diagnosis. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-stephanie-m-wytovich)

## Alicia Steinmann![Meet Alicia Steinmann](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776958618775-personal_1776958618361_1776958618361_alicia_steinmann_bio-headshot-alicia.jpeg)

The most meaningful project I’ve worked on in New York is Frayed Knot Collective’s production of <i>Seminar</i> by Theresa Rebeck at the Off-Off-Broadway IATI Theater. Written by a woman, directed by a woman, and mostly produced by women, working on <i>Seminar</i> was a true joy. So often in this industry, it feels impossible to find roles for women that speak to us from the heart. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-alicia-steinmann)

## Sammm![Meet Sammm](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776924559519-personal_1776924559038_1776924559038_sammm_d381e283-9528-4c2c-9830-de2acdedec68.png)

All of my projects have been meaningful to me in one way or another. I think the most meaningful project to me at this time would be my song ‘legos to the moon.’ It is my most recent single release of last year. I started writing this song about 2 years ago, after going on a first date with someone new. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-sammm)
