## Children’s Online Privacy Code

From 31 March to 5 June 2026, the OAIC is seeking feedback from children, young people, parents and carers, as well as industry, civil society, academia, and other interested parties, on the Exposure Draft of the Children’s Online Privacy Code (the Code).


Our Privacy for Kids hub has helpful resources about the Code, including workbooks and child-friendly guides, as well as instructions on how to participate in the public consultation.


[Learn more about the Children’s Online Privacy Code](https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/privacy-for-kids)
