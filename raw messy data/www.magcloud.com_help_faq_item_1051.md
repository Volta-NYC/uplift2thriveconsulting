## What formats can I distribute my MagCloud publication in?

MagCloud enables you to publish a magazine-format publication in print or as a digital version. [Print versions](https://www.magcloud.com/products/print) are available in a variety of sizes and binding options. [Digital versions](https://www.magcloud.com/products/digital) can be read through the MagCloud web viewer, or as a downloaded PDF on any desktop or mobile device.
