Being a business owner isn’t easy. There are amazing highs, but the lows are often quite intense as well. Somedays it feels like the problems never stop – regardless of how much success you achieve. The problems just grow, evolve, morph. So we asked business owners if they sometimes wonder what it would be like to just have a regular job and have shared some of the highlights below.

## Dawn Duncan![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778728690240-personal_1778728689590_1778728689590_dawn_duncan_dawn-duncan--768x976.jpg)

All entrepreneurs face challenges and I don’t know any who haven’t failed at least at one thing (or 100). However, I also believe that most business owners will tell you they are happy, at least happy in the sense that they don’t long for a regular job. Real entrepreneurs don’t relate to regular jobs; they find them dull and confining for the most part. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-dawn-duncan)

## Orinthia Carter![Meet Orinthia Carter](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777932913213-personal_1777932912746_1777932912746_orinthia_carter_img_8172.png)

Am I happier as a business owner? I wouldn’t say happier… I would say grateful. There was a moment—not long ago—where I sat in silence after a long day of work. No guaranteed paycheck. No safety net. Just me, my vision, and the responsibility of everything I’ve built. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-orinthia-carter)

## Gabriella Briggs![Meet Gabriella Briggs](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777521610309-personal_1777521609740_1777521609740_gabriella_briggs_gwyke-portrait-2025-1.jpg)

I love this question because I currently I have both a business and a “regular” job! However, it was not always like this. Let me give you some context before I dive right in. I graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design in 2020 with a BFA in Photography, during the COVID-19 pandemic. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-gabriella-briggs)

## Vanessa Watterson![Meet Vanessa Watterson](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778081009824-personal_1778081008933_1778081008933_vanessa_watterson_chatgpt-image-may-6-2026-11_23_19-am.png)

Absolutely. There are definitely moments where I think, “Wow… a normal job would be easier.” Especially in the early years. Before Elderberry Boost, I was a teacher. I actually loved teaching. This wasn’t one of those stories where I was miserable and desperately trying to escape my career. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-vanessa-watterson)

## Nadiya Qureshi![Meet Nadiya Qureshi](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777542577447-personal_1777542577674_1777542577674_nadiya_qureshi_whatsapp-image-2026-04-30-at-45550-am.jpeg)

Honestly, it’s been a bit of a rollercoaster. There are days when I feel really stressed about operations, sales, and how everything is going to scale, and in those moments I do catch myself thinking, ‘life would have been simpler if I had just stayed in a regular job.’ But then something unexpected happens. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nadiya-qureshi)

## Hillary Gillis![Meet Hillary Gillis](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777390501857-personal_1777390501295_1777390501295_hillary_gillis_img_9256.png)

I’m a very happy business owner, and I know full well that I’m practically incapable of a “normal” job. I have bad days and difficult clients, like anyone else, but because of the nature of my work most of my client interactions are fairly brief and when it’s over, it’s over. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-hillary-gillis)
