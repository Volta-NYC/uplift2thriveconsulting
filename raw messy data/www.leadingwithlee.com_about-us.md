## ABOUT US

Leading with Lee™ an award-winning digital and print resource publication for emerging and seasoned entrepreneurs, trendsetters, and game changers. It's where boss creatives network, gain visibility, and learn how to start and grow their businesses or personal brands. Our readers have 24-hour access to a plethora of career and branding tips ranging from our exclusive guest interviews to our informative articles. Since 2014, we helped and partnered with brands to successfully gain exposure and meaningful connections for their businesses and personal brands.

​

In addition, we cover pop culture, fashion, entertainment, and community News.
