## Ayana Harris![Meet Ayana Harris](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779226419872-personal_1779226413857_1779226413857_ayana_harris_1000024832.jpg)

The kindest thing anyone has ever done for me was give me a second chance before I was even old enough to understand what that truly meant. I was adopted at three months old. My birth parents’ relationship was very complicated and, from what I understand, unhealthy in many ways. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-ayana-harris)

## Tara Bennethum![Meet Tara Bennethum](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779322894579-personal_1779322894468_1779322894468_tara_bennethum_ty-4-being-you.jpg)

When I was five years old, I was hospitalized for a significant stretch of time. Being that young in a sterile, clinical environment is terrifying; you feel entirely disconnected, helpless, and small. One afternoon, a hospital volunteer walked into my room carrying a homemade, crocheted\ felt red ‘Brave Heart Lion’ Care Bear. She didn’t know me, but she chose to see me. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tara-bennethum)

## BILLY QUINN![Meet BILLY QUINN](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779171689562-personal_1779171688927_1779171688927_billy_quinn_img_0864.jpg)

The kindest thing anyone has ever done for me, was die for me. I was dead in my sin, slave to it. Engineered by it, I became a child of wrath consumed by ego. Even in my mothers womb I was shaped in iniquity, and in sin my mother conceived me. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-billy-quinn)

## L. Dawn King, MPA![Meet L. Dawn King, MPA](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778943921689-personal_1778943921974_1778943921974_ldawn_kingmpa_advertisement-1.jpg)
