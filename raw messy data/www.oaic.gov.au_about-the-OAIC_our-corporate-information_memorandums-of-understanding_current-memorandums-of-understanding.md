# Current memorandums of understanding

## Below are our current financial and non-financial memorandums of understanding (MOUs).

Domestic MOUs and exchanges of letter set out the way we’ll work in partnership with other agencies and may also provide funding for specific services.

Older MOUs can be found on [Trove](https://webarchive.nla.gov.au/awa/20190509024107/https://www.oaic.gov.au/about-us/corporate-information/mous/).
