There is so much to learn from investments that went really well as well as those that went really poorly and so we asked some of the wisest creatives and entrepreneurs in the community to tell us the stories of their best and worst investments.

## Holly Gort

My best investment wasn’t a tool or a marketing strategy—it was people. I’ve been incredibly fortunate to find my tribe: my husband, my mentor, and the team I get to work with every day. I’m a creative with big ideas, and my husband brings the balance I need—he’s supportive, but he also helps me think through the real-life implications of those ideas. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-holly-gort)

## Tessa Ringlein![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1773930793347-personal_1773930792914_1773930792914_tessa_ringlein_dsc_4612.jpg)

Honestly, the best investment I’ve made has been in myself. Learning to trust my creative instincts, value my work, and embrace my own style completely changed the way I show up—not just as a photographer, but as a business owner. When I stopped second-guessing and started creating from a place of confidence, everything else began to grow more naturally. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tessa-ringlein)

## Alice Terrell![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774151644390-personal_1774151644031_1774151644031_alice_terrell_img_7670.jpeg)

Oh man, these hips worst decision I’ve made in this journey is putting money into what is trending vs. what I’m into. It was not my lane, definitely not my thing and when it then trend ended, I was looking for the next thing. I decided to take a hiatus to find myself, to find my passion. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-alice-terrell)

## 1KSpin![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774155666710-personal_1774155666308_1774155666308_kspin_photo-output.png)

My best investment was betting on myself. No one else will invest in you until you invest in yourself. Investing in myself has truly been a blessing because I never saw myself developing to the point I’m at currently. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-1kspin)

## Jay Zeager![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774480272422-personal_1774480272665_1774480272665_jay_zeager_jay-zeager-presentation.jpg)

The best investment I’ve ever made was going to the Florida Auctioneer Academy. I had already done a couple auctions for free and another auctioneer suggested I get licensed and I could go to work with him. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-jay-zeager-2)

## Lolly Peterson![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774300526902-personal_1774300526438_1774300526438_lolly_peterson_img_9356.jpeg)

I made many investments such as, real estate, e-commerces, and selling cars. Only to learn what I thought was quick money. Of course I knew with these investments, I had to advertise, invest more money and socialize with people. But I learned knowledge and a valuable lesson, stop chasing money and invest in a business you love. Did I make money from these investments? [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-lolly-peterson)

## Wanda Lopez![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774566862474-personal_1774566862357_1774566862357_wanda_4a8ba136-e8e5-4b3c-8376-c33438f3751e.jpg)

When most people hear the word investment, they immediately think of money; saving it, spending it, or growing it. But one of the most powerful investments we can make has nothing to do with dollars. It’s the time we choose to give ourselves and the people we love. Time shapes our present, influences our future, and once it’s gone, we don’t get it back. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-wanda-lopez-2)

## Sarah Schlievert![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1773942972414-personal_1773942971761_1773942971761_sarah_schlievert_smrphotography-317.jpg)

When I opened my fitness business in Ohio in 2013, one of the first things that struck me was how unregulated the industry really is. You can call yourself a personal trainer, a nutrition coach, or a yoga teacher without any formal education or certification at all. That never sat right with me. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-sarah-schlievert)

## Brody Dixon

The best investment I’ve made as the owner of RedHairs is in myself—believing in my vision, trusting the process, and putting in the work. Without that self-belief, none of it would have happened. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-brody-dixon)

## Charlotte Smith![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774365505891-personal_1774365505491_1774365505491_charlotte_smith_img_7995.jpeg)

Whenever I was doing a tedious task such as cutting paper or applying labels to my lip balms, I always tried to think of a way to improve the process since time is money. If I could speed up the process, my profits would increase. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-charlotte-smith-3)

## Melissa Arey![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1773863106873-personal_1773863106453_1773863106453_melissa_arey_img-73.jpg)

The best investment for my business, hands down, has been my printers! I have three different printers and though a big initial investment it truly makes the difference in being able to offer high quality printing on many different paper types, textures and thicknesses. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-melissa-arey-2)

## Ramon Gil![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774123369243-personal_1774123368768_1774123368768_ramon_gil_ramon_teaching.jpg)

A few years ago, I launched my own marketing design company called Fresh Concentrate. We specialized in reaching multicultural audiences. Prior to this I had worked as a senior art director at an ad agency and as a creative director at a non-profit. The best investment I ever made was in my own education as an entrepreneur. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-ramon-gil-2)

## Scott Field![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1774113493251-personal_1774113492841_1774113492841_scott_field_26-27-scott-sincere-headshot.jpg)

I have a couple of stories that go in both directions, best, and worst! I’ll start with the worst investments. Our first was spending a large amount of money upfront for a person who would help create a really good sketch comedy show for our venue. This person had ALL of the qualifications. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-scott-field)

## Gina DeSantis![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c-1773926621149-personal_1773926620605_1773926620605_gina_desantis_ginadesantis_250327_0028.jpg)

The worst investment I have made for my business is hiring a PR team to promote my artwork and secure press. I had reached out to an all female team after a very condescending conversation with a male marketing agent in the Cleveland area. I wanted to help launch my brand fully into a national arena. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-gina-desantis-2)
