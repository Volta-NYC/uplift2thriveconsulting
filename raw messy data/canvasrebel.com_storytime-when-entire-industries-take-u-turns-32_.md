There’s so much to learn from times when there are industry wide U-Turns and so we asked so we asked the community to share stories of such reversals that they’ve witnessed over the course of their careers.

## Tarryn Hoff![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778787221618-personal_1778787221464_1778787221464_tarryn_hoff_3940877_orig.jpg)

One of the biggest industry U-turns I’ve witnessed over the course of my career has been the shift from “exercise for appearance” to “exercise for longevity and quality of life.” When I first started personal training in the late 1990s, much of the fitness industry revolved around aesthetics—how lean someone looked, how much weight they could lose quickly, or achieving the “perfect” physique. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tarryn-hoff-3)

## Kelly Flannery![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778594798591-personal_1778594798460_1778594798460_kelly_flannery_img_7740.jpg)

One of the biggest industry-wide U-turns I’ve experienced has been the shift from viewing chambers of commerce primarily as networking organizations to recognizing them as strategic community conveners and advocates. Earlier in my career, success was often measured by attendance numbers, ribbon cuttings, and traditional business mixers. While those things still matter, the expectations of members and the broader community have changed dramatically. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kelly-flannery-2)

## Ryan Furman![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778615430524-personal_1778615430393_1778615430393_ryan_furman_headhsot.jpg)

One of the biggest industry-wide U-turns I’ve experienced in real estate over the last 25 years has been the shift from relationship-driven business to algorithm-driven business — and now, interestingly, back toward relationships again. When I started in real estate, the business was heavily built on personal connections, local expertise, referrals, and face-to-face communication. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-ryan-furman-2)

## Victor Lund![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778084412323-personal_1778084411866_1778084411866_victor_lund_img_0364.jpeg)

Real Estate is testing new strategies for property marketing that challenge the portal dominance. AI may play a role in that. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-victor-lund)
