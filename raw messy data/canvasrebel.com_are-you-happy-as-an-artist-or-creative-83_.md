Pursuing a creative or artistic career can certainly be fulfilling, but it’s far from certain. Over the years, we’ve heard about the struggles from thousands of artists and creatives – it’s incredibly challenging and it can be tempting to wonder – what if you hadn’t pursued this path. We asked artists and creatives from across the community if they’ve every had those doubts themselves.

## Victoria Cornejo![Meet Victoria Cornejo](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778126648316-personal_1778126648574_1778126648574_victoria_cornejo__dsf8466.jpg)

This question is something on my mind right now. I am currently balancing both worlds: I work a grueling food service job for 8 hours and come home and write screenplays and attempt to edit the documentary I have promised to finally finish this summer. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-victoria-cornejo)

## Mbangala Perrott Bénédicte![Meet Mbangala Perrott Bénédicte](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777913961764-personal_1777913961083_1777913961083_mbangalaperrott_bndicte_867eafb8-dbcd-4074-9520-9ceb9fecc1e0.jpg)

Am I happier as a business owner? I’m not sure I would define myself that way. I feel deeply fulfilled as someone who creates, someone who allows space for art, for thought, for emotion to exist and circulate in the world. That is where my happiness lives. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-mbangala-perrott-benedicte)

## Tristin Killian![Meet Tristin Killian](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778023022588-personal_1778023010767_1778023010767_tristin_killian_049a0221-2-2.jpg)

I am definitely happier working as a creative. That said, working in an industry where your work is constantly evaluated by others can be challenging, and maintaining a positive, confident mindset takes effort. Like many creatives, Ive had moments where i wondered what it would be like to step into a more traditional, stable role. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-tristin-killian)

## Olivia Wong![Meet Olivia Wong](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778018424826-personal_1778018424392_1778018424392_olivia_wong_dsc06566.jpg)

I’m incredibly happy, and deeply grateful, to be a full-time creative. The truth is, I don’t really think about what it would be like to have a “regular job”, which has given me a lot of clarity. It’s how I know I’m exactly where I’m meant to be. I remember my first meeting with an industry professional, back when I was in high school. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-olivia-wong-3)

## Ana María Barceló![Meet Ana María Barceló](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778771989917-personal_1778771989466_1778771989466_anamara_barcel_2b3c074c-ca4b-4b0e-befe-2278fb1cfcc2.jpg)

I feel incredibly fulfilled as a dancer because dance has been part of my identity for as long as I can remember. I began dancing at the age of three, and from that moment, movement, music, and performance became a natural language for me. I truly cannot imagine my life without dance. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-ana-maria-barcelo)

## Sarah Frazier![Meet Sarah Frazier](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776877359563-personal_1776877358986_1776877358986_sarah_frazier_sarah-2.jpg)

The quick response is that I couldn’t imagine doing anything else, I am absolutely happy as an artist! But mindset is truly everything, working in the theater industry has high high’s and low low’s. During the lows, I can understand the draw to a more consistent profession, but I know a regular job with no artistic outlet is not for me. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-sarah-frazier)

## Madstone Rowan![Meet Madstone Rowan](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777473098840-personal_1777473098302_1777473098302_madstone_rowan_madstone-pop_v3.jpg)

I’ve had ‘regular’ jobs – but I was always thinking of music. I would write songs in my head through the workday. So I never really think about going back there. Regular jobs are jobs, careers – being an artist is a calling. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-madstone-rowan)

## Andrew Vogt![Meet Andrew Vogt](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1776863370679-personal_1776863370260_1776863370260_andrew_vogt_vid_20250626_121132_444-1.jpg)

Yes, I am genuinely happy as a creative. Photography and videography have been life changing, not just because of the work, but because of the people I have met and the opportunities that have come from it. It gives me a sense of purpose and freedom that is hard to replicate elsewhere. Truly am grateful for everything. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-andrew-vogt-2)
