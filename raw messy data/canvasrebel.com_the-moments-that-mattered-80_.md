Every once in a while, something happens that really matters. Something that will define at least the next chapter of your journey – perhaps it’s a conversation with a client, a meeting with a visionary or a major event in your personal life. Below, you’ll find some very insightful folks sharing defining moments from their journeys.

## Austin Moore![Meet Austin Moore](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778945028953-personal_1778945028646_1778945028646_austin_moore_img_9984.jpg)

I had been working in autism for 12 years – since 2001 – with an individual family and their child who had autism. He was nonspeaking and use very minimal repeated words for requests. We tried all modalities of therapy to best accommodate his needs. Endless hours of applied behavioral analysis (ABA therapy), speech, OT, PT etc. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-austin-moore-2)

## Lisa Galardi-Brugueras![Meet Lisa Galardi-Brugueras](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779233321212-personal_1779233320823_1779233320823_lisa_galardibrugueras_chatgpt-image-apr-22-2026-07_58_51-pm.png)

The defining moment for me to enter into the health and wellness field happened when I took a nasty fall and badly hurt my knee at the school I worked at. I had my doctor check it out and luckily nothing was broken or torn. She said that my knee will heal but she wanted me to come back for more tests on my lungs. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-lisa-galardi-brugueras)

## Eva Salomon![Meet Eva Salomon](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779123765435-personal_1779123765305_1779123765305_eva_salomon_a50c1e02-b930-4da2-bb73-83c7667574d9.png)

Yes, there was definitely a defining moment in my professional career, although at the time I did not fully understand that it was changing the direction of my life. For many years, I built a successful career in the corporate world, especially in sales, logistics, and business development. I was used to performing, achieving, solving problems, and constantly moving forward. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-eva-salomon)

## Aleighsha Webber![Meet Aleighsha Webber](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779123322067-personal_1779123321876_1779123321876_aleighsha_webber_professional-photo-1.jpg)

One of the most defining moments in my professional career occurred before my therapy career began. While serving on active duty in the United States Air Force, I was accepted into graduate school for Clinical Mental Health Counseling. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-aleighsha-webber)

## Halea Brooks

A defining moment that changed the trajectory of my professional career and life is the death of my sister. Victoria was just graduating high school and on endevers to find a career when everyones lives changed in just an instance. Her life was taken by public transportation, RTA. A brand most Americans are familiar with and use often. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-halea-brooks)

## Sheree Beaumont![Meet Sheree Beaumont](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778820822232-personal_1778820821823_1778820821823_sheree_beaumont_fun.jpg)

The moment that genuinely changed the trajectory of everything, came not from a professional milestone, but from a personal collapse that happened right in the middle of building my career. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-sheree-beaumont)

## Susie Moya, LCSW![Meet Susie Moya, LCSW](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778865180134-personal_1778865180291_1778865180291_susie_moyalcsw_susie-m-bio-pic-.jpg)

My background is in social work and non-profit work providing trauma-informed services. I transitioned to private practice nearly a decade ago. I started off at a group practice and within a year, I knew I wanted to start my own practice. At this point of my life, all I knew was heavy complex trauma work. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-susie-moya-lcsw)

## James Longobardi  ![Meet James Longobardi](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779199936222-personal_1779199935765_1779199935765_james_longobardi_dr-longobardis-professional-picture-jpeg.jpeg)

One defining moment in my professional career was actually the decision to completely change the direction of my life and start over professionally at age 36. I knew I wanted to become a doctor from the time I was about eight years old, but my path was not traditional or easy. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-james-longobardi)

## Dan Hartmann![Meet Dan Hartmann](https://canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778329615811-personal_1778329615581_1778329615581_dan_hartmann_8302027-10.jpeg)

One defining moment that completely changed the trajectory of my life and career was watching one of my close friends become severely ill. Over the course of about a year, I watched him deteriorate physically, mentally, emotionally, and spiritually. Despite seeking conventional care, it felt like no one was truly addressing the deeper root causes of what he was experiencing. That experience deeply impacted me. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-dan-hartmann)
