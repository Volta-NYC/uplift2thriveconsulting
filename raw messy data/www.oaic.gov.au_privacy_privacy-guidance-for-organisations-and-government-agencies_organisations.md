# Organisations

## If the _Privacy Act 1988_ covers your organisation, you need to understand your obligations when handling personal information. The Privacy Act covers organisations with an annual turnover of more than $3 million and some other organisations.

A small business or not-for-profit not covered by the Privacy Act may choose to opt-in to the Privacy Act, see those that have on our [Privacy opt-in register](https://www.oaic.gov.au/2022/privacy/privacy-registers/privacy-opt-in-register).
