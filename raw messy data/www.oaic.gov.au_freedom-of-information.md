# Freedom of information

## The _Freedom of Information Act 1982_ (FOI Act) gives you the right to request access to government-held information. This includes information they hold about you or about government policies and decisions.

See the [FOI Act](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Series/C2004A02562) on the Federal Register of Legislation.
