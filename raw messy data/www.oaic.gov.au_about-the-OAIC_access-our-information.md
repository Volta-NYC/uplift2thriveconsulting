# Access our information

You can access information we hold by:

- seeking administrative access to documents we hold
- making a freedom of information request seeking access to documents
- view a range of data and statical information published by the OAIC
- viewing information in documents accessed under the _Freedom of Information Act 1982_ (FOI Act) in our FOI disclosure log
- accessing information about our operations via the Information Publication Scheme.
