## Social Media Minimum Age: Entity contact details

If you’re concerned your [personal information](https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/your-privacy-rights/your-personal-information/what-is-personal-information) has been mishandled, you should first complain to the age-restricted social media platform or third party you think has mishandled it.

The OAIC has compiled a list of the contact details of [named age-restricted social media platforms \- external site](https://www.esafety.gov.au/about-us/industry-regulation/social-media-age-restrictions/which-platforms-are-age-restricted) and common age assurance providers to assist individuals to enquire directly to entities that their complaint relates.
