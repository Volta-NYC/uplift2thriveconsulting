# Delegation of human resources powers and functions

[Listen](https://app-oc.readspeaker.com/cgi-bin/rsent?customerid=9755&lang=en_au&readclass=page-content&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.oaic.gov.au%2Fabout-the-OAIC%2Four-corporate-information%2Foperational-information%2Fdelegation-of-human-resources-powers-and-functions "Listen to this page using ReadSpeaker webReader")

Published: 23 March 2026

1 min read

The human resources and integrity delegation provides the powers and functions for particular sections under the:

- Long Service Leave (Commonwealth Employees) Act 1976
- Maternity Leave (Commonwealth Employees) Act 1973
- Public Service Act 1999
- Public Service Regulations 2023
- Public Service Classification Rules 2000
- Office of the Australian Information Commissioner Enterprise Agreement
- Safety, Rehabilitation and Compensation Act 1988
- Public Interest Disclosure Act 2013
- Australian Public Service Directions 2022
- National Ant- Corruption Commission Act 2022.
