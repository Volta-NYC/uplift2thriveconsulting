Incepting his career in 2005 Lunden Olin, known as Looks By Lunden is an internationally renowned celebrity wardrobe stylist. Hailing from Brooklyn, New York, Lunden’s multifaceted expertise ranges as a creative director, personal shopper, and an esteemed influencer.

Lunden commenced his career for the legendary New York Fashion Week in 2005. It was there Lunden debuted his eclectique-metropolitan merged collection entitled "Olin Edizione”.

​

As the advancement of his brand prevailed throughout the duration of his métier his celebrity consumers expanded. Lunden’s celebrity clientele and agency affiliation included significant talent such as Lil Nas X, Reginae Carter, Fivio Foreign, Dreamdoll, Ginuwine, Big Freedia, rapper Lil' Mama, Amara La Negra, rapper Desiigner, TobiasTruvillion of FOX  “Empire”, Vicky Jeudy of “Orange is the New Black”, Safaree of LHH New York & LA, Mariahlynn and more!
