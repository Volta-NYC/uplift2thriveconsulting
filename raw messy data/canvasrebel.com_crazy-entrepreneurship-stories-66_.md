We love grabbing drinks with our entrepreneur friends because they always have the craziest stories. Sometimes it’s a big, unexpected win. Other times it’s a problem that came out of nowhere and couldn’t possibly have been foreseen. There is so much to learn from these stories and so we asked some of the brightest entrepreneurs and creatives in the community to share some of those stories below.

## Katelyn Jones ![](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778259564572-1778259564491_katelyn_jones_img_1524.jpg)

Being a business owner is one of the most difficult things I have ever done. While this story may maybe isn’t wild on it’s own it changed the entire trajectory of my business and I think it’s important to share how something that can be seen as negative can be completely life altering for you. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-katelyn-jones-2)

## Julia Mason![Meet Julia Mason](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1777908516470-personal_1777908516020_1777908516020_julia_mason_julia-branding-5926-11-web.jpg)

One of the craziest, most life-altering moments in my business came in the form of something I never planned for — a surprise fourth pregnancy. At the time, I felt like I had finally found a rhythm. My business was growing, my offers were clear, and I had three young children at home. We had even gotten rid of all our baby things. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-julia-mason-2)

## Patrick Schober![Meet Patrick Schober](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778487929922-personal_1778487929230_poetica-team-shots-36.jpg)

In the digital marketing space, clients are occasionally hit by two pressing questions at once: 1. Does this actually make me money? 2. How the heck does this even work? Both of these are stressful, but sometimes the confusion behind No. 2 is the most damaging. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-patrick-schober-2)

## Angela Gentlewolf![Meet Angela Gentlewolf](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778407354777-personal_1778407354629_1778407354629_angela_gentlewolf_org_dsc08321.jpeg)

One of the most underappreciated parts of entrepreneurship is that nobody really tells you how much of it is just figuring things out in real time. People often see the finished product — the brand, the training, the social media, the dogs, the growth — but not the chaos happening behind the scenes while you build it. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-angela-gentlewolf)

## Dr. Tamara Johnson – Carter![Meet Dr. Tamara Johnson – Carter](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778560639505-personal_1778560639381_1778560639381_drtamara_johnsoncarter_tamaraj-50th-black-dress-phto.jpg)

One of the craziest and most devastating things that’s ever happened to me as a salon owner was the fire that destroyed my salon. It’s still hard to describe because it didn’t just damage a business — it completely disrupted a life I had spent years building. The salon wasn’t just a workplace to me. It was something I poured myself into every single day. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-dr-tamara-johnson-carter)
