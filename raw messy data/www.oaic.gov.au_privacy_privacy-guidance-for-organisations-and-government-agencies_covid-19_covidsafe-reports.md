# COVIDSafe reports

## Below are our 6-month reports on the Commissioner's COVIDSafe powers and functions.

Since 16 August 2022 the COVIDSafe app was no longer required to prevent or control the entry, emergence, establishment or spread of COVID-19 in Australia.
