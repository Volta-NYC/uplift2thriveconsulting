# Help: Contact Us

- [Ask MagCloud](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq "Ask MagCloud")
- [Contact Us](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact "Contact Us")


Before contacting us, check the [Ask MagCloud](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq) section for common questions we get asked. For information about making a PDF to publish, see the [Create](https://www.magcloud.com/create) page.


After contacting us, a member of our support team will respond via email within 24 hours, Monday through Friday 9am to 5pm PST.


- ## What can we help you with?

  - [I need to make changes to a recent order.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/0)
  - [I'm having trouble making a purchase.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/1)
  - [My order has not arrived.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/2)

  - [I received my order but there is a problem with it.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/3)
  - [I need help creating/uploading my PDF.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/4)
  - [I want a price quote.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/5)
  - [I'm a publisher and have a question about sales/payments.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/6)
  - [I want to print something MagCloud does not appear to support.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/7)

  - [I want to leave general feedback for MagCloud.](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/8)
  - [I'm having trouble or have a question about something else](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/9)
