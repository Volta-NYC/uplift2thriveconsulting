Overwhelmingly, media coverage of team building, hiring, training, etc. is often geared towards the issues faced by HR at Fortune 500 sized companies. Even when it’s positioned as advice for small business owners it is often coming from an executive or consultant working primarily with giant corporations. We think it’s far more relevant to hear from small and medium business owners who have successfully grown their businesses – and so we asked some very talented folks to tell us the stories of their initial hires and the struggles and lessons they can share.

## Carlyn Bushman![Meet Carlyn Bushman](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779222097662-personal_1779222097298_1779222097298_carlyn_bushman_carlyn-26-176.jpg)

I started my first business — a handbag brand — while I was still working in corporate, so in the beginning it was just me balancing both worlds at the same time. That experience taught me so much about entrepreneurship, operations, branding, and what it really takes to build something from the ground up. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-carlyn-bushman-2)

## Nasir Zahir![Meet Nasir Zahir](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779132257083-personal_1779132256826_1779132256826_nasir_zahir_nz1.png)

I started completely on my own right here in Dallas, TX just me, my vision, and a deep passion for the hospitality industry. Those early solo days as a hotel consultant were both thrilling and humbling. Every decision landed on my desk, every mistake was mine to own, and every small win felt like a major victory. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-nasir-zahir)

## Terrance Pittman![Meet Terrance Pittman](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1779230425318-personal_1779230425188_1779230425188_terrance_pittman_img_0239.jpeg)

When I first started Drip Team Prints￼, it was just me doing everything — teaching classes, prepping materials, cleaning, marketing, and responding to customers. In the beginning, you really have to wear every hat while trying to build something people believe in. It was a lot of long days, but seeing people enjoy the creative experience made it worth it. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-terrance-pittman)

## Kaylan Mitchell![Meet Kaylan Mitchell](https://cdn.canvasrebel.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/c-1778870805273-personal_1778870805060_1778870805060_kaylan_mitchell_screen-shot-2026-01-08-at-41514-pm.png)

Our business did not have employees from day one! In the early days, my co-owner Lindsey and I would rotate 3-4 days in the store and 2 days working in the background per week. However, retail clothing store demands being what they are, we made the leap to hire our first employee about 2 years in. [Read More>>](https://canvasrebel.com/meet-kaylan-mitchell-2)
