## Magcloud & Blurb

**1-3**

[Who is Blurb?](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq/item/9448)

As of May 5, 2014, Magcloud became part of [Blurb](http://www.blurb.com/). Blurb is an indie publishing platform, founded on the belief that authors shouldn't have to worry about the output; it's the input—the work—that matters. Blurb provides magazine—and book-making tools, helpful content to guide authors through the creation process, and marketing support to showcase, sell and distribute your books. And since you're probably already creating and marketing your magazine, we think this is truly the start of a beautiful friendship.

[What changes should I be aware of?](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq/item/9447)

For now, nothing is changing.

Your experience should continue exactly as it has with MagCloud all along:

-Your publications will look exactly the same

-They will be printed at the same location

-The urls to promote and distribute your content will be the same

-You can publish new content via MagCloud

-Prices will be the same

-Your earnings will be paid to you in the same way

[How can I keep in touch or get help?](https://www.magcloud.com/help/faq/item/9449)

We’d love to hear from you. Please contact Customer Support [here](https://www.magcloud.com/help/contact/contactform/8).

Make sure you have signed up for our [newsletter](https://www.magcloud.com/account) to receive updates (under Updates, News and Offers in Your Account settings).
